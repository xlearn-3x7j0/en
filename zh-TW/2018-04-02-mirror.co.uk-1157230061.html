The Masters 2018 odds: Betting special as Woods and McIlroy eye Green Jacket

It's the most open Masters in years with a host of big-time players at the top of the market to pull on the Green Jacket at the glorious Augusta.

Tiger Woods is back in contention and there is a strong contingent of European players, including Rory McIlroy and defending champion Sergio Garcia.

The Americans are strong though, with a new generation set to dominate the game, alongside world No.1 Dustin Johnson and former Masters champion Jordan Spieth.

Justin Thomas has also had an incredible 18 months, including the win at the USPGA and the FedEx Cup to close out a historic 2017.

More of the same can be expected this season having already won on the Tour, but there is plenty of value further down at longer odds - let's take a look at the best prices with less than two weeks to go.
