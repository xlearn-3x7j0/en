Manchester United striker Romelu Lukaku is one of the most prominent strikers in the Premier League but that doesn't stop him from running his own errands.

Lukaku purchased a few items from his local Tesco in Cheshire on Wednesday and left the shop without a bag.

The 24-year-old was driving his £250,000 black and red Rolls-Royce Wraith.

Lukaku has endured some difficult moments at United but has been impressive recently

Lukaku was driving his black and red Rolls-Royce Wraith, which cost £250,000

Lukaku has endured some difficult moments at times this season following his £75million summer switch from Everton, but the Belgian has still managed to score 26 goals in 45 games across all competitions.

Of late Lukaku has been in impressive form as he has scored in consecutive games against Sevilla, Brighton and Swansea.

On Saturday he will be hoping to extend that sequence as United face Manchester City away in a game that could see their bitter rivals crowned Premier League champions.

Renowned for his strength and hold-up play, Lukaku is superb at bursting past opposing defenders, although his finishing can lack conviction at times.

Although their Champions League hopes are over and they are set to miss out on the league title, there is still a chance that Lukaku's first season at United could end in glory if he helps them to win the FA Cup.
