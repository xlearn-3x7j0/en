The Renewal Diet 

The Renewal Diet consists of a weekly cycle of meals and beverages to consume each day with one day being a cheat day

Breakfast meals are swapped out with smoothies rich in antioxidants and drinks are complemented with tablespoons of 'Activated Phenolics'

Lunch and dinners consist of vegetables and lean meats

Dr Candrawinata said the diet encourages people to eat more fresh fruit and berries which are rich in antioxidants as well as supplement their daily routine with 'Activated Phenolics' a powder created by Renovatio - a company he founded. 

'In short ''The Renewal Diet'' helps people to renew and restore their cells, supporting their health and wellbeing and reducing the risk of chronic disease,' he explained. 

'It also supports weight loss and increases energy and alertness levels,' he added.
