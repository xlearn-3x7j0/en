Tottenham's Dele Alli reveals first crush and school fields marriage

  • Dele Alli has revealed his first crush and admits they used to marry at school
  • Alli's crush lived on the same council estate as him in Milton Keynes
  • The revelation comes as part of a campaign to challenge male stereotypes 

Published: 06:34 EDT, 5 April 2018 | Updated: 06:48 EDT, 5 April 2018

Dele Alli has revealed when he had his first crush and admits they even used to get married on the fields at school when he was a youngster.

The Tottenham star grew up on a council estate in Milton Keynes and that was where he would first see his childhood crush.

Alli revealed some of his most embarrassing moments as part of Lynx's You Gold campaign.  

Dele Alli (right) reveals embarrassing moments with Michael Dapaah (centre) and Not3s (left)

He was joined by comedian Michael Dapaah and rapper Not3s as they all reflected on some of the most awkward moments in their lives in Lynx's bid to challenge male stereotypes.

Alli said: 'My first crush was when I was six or seven, there was a girl that lived on the other end of my council estate and we used to pretend to get married and everything, either in a field or our school field. 

'We got married a couple of times, and I even remember one time I went there and I ran off as I didn't want to do it.'

Alli (left) tells all on his first crush and how they used to get married in the school fields

Alli silenced some of his critics last weekend as he played a vital role in Tottenham's 3-1 win over Chelsea.

The 21-year-old scored two goals for his side as they moved eight points clear of Chelsea and closer to nailing down a Champions League spot.

He will be back in action this weekend when Tottenham travel to Stoke on Saturday.
