Ray Wilkins tributes and reaction as football world pays respect

Ray Wilkins was a man treasured not only by the clubs he graced but by the whole footballing world.

The much-loved player passed away in hospital after suffering a heart attack at his home last week - he was just 61.

After starting out at Chelsea, the cultured midfielder enjoyed a stellar career at the top of the game at clubs like Manchester United and AC Milan.

He also played at two World Cups for England before enjoying a managerial career at Fulham and QPR before returning to Chelsea as assistant boss.

Many will know him from his role as a pundit on Sky Sport and Talksport.

This has lead to an out-pouring of emotion from players and fans.
