Ray Wilkins dies aged 61

Last Updated: 04/04/18 2:14pm

Former England, Chelsea and Manchester United midfielder Ray Wilkins has died at the age of 61.

Wilkins, who was capped 84 times by his country, suffered a heart attack last week and passed away in hospital on Wednesday morning.

Born on September 14, 1956, in Hillingdon, Wilkins represented his country in two World Cups and also played for AC Milan, Paris Saint-Germain, Rangers and Queens Park Rangers in his club career, before retiring in 1997.
