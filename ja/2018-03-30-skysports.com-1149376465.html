Salford seal 32-16 Super League win over Catalans Dragons

By Sky Sports Rugby League

Last Updated: 30/03/18 2:53pm

Salford made it three successive home wins in Super League with victory over bottom-of-the-table Catalans Dragons.

Tries from Jake Bibby and Niall Evalds gave Salford an early foothold but Fouad Yaha replied to reduce Catalans' deficit to 12-4 at the break.

Greg Johnson extended the Red Devils' lead before Yaha ran the length of the pitch to keep the Dragons in the contest.

But further tries followed from Josh Jones and Kris Welham before Greg Bird's last-minute consolation score.
