Full list of the 30 runners and riders at Irish Grand National 2018

It is almost time for the biggest race on the Irish horse racing calendar.

The Boylesports Irish Grand National is set to go off at 5pm on Easter Monday at Fairyhouse, in County Meath.

Ireland's richest steeplechase will see 30 runners speed around the three miles and five furlong course and 24 fences - which is going heavy.

Pairofbrowneyes is set to go off at favourites with Monbeg Notorious and Mall Dini also among the pre-race front runners.
