DIRECTOR'S DEALS: Restaurant Group boss Andy McCue

Published: 18:39 EDT, 3 April 2018 | Updated: 18:39 EDT, 3 April 2018

The chief executive of The Restaurant Group has snapped up more than £114,000 of shares in the firm.

Andy McCue has bought 44,971 shares at nearly £2.54 each in the owner of Frankie & Benny’s and Mexican chain Chiquito.

McCue joined as chief executive in September 2016 from bookmaker Paddy Power, where he was also chief executive. 

Before joining Paddy Power, McCue, who is also a non-executive director of Hostelworld Group, worked for OC&C Consultants.
