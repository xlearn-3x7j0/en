Danny Simpson reveals his PFA Team of the Year with SEVEN City stars

  • Danny Simpson posted a picture of his selections for the PFA awards on Twitter 
  • Leicester defender voted for Liverpool star Mo Salah who is currently top scorer 
  • His PFA Team of the Year has seven Manchester City players in the line-up 

Published: 05:24 EDT, 30 March 2018 | Updated: 08:34 EDT, 30 March 2018

Danny Simpson has posted his PFA Team of the Year selection on Twitter, also revealing his selection for the league's best player is Mo Salah.  

The Leicester defender picked seven players from Manchester City with Pep Guardiola's side dominating this season. 

Sergio Aguero, Raheem Sterling, Leroy Sane, David Silva, Kevin De Bruyne, Nicolas Otamendi and Kyle Walker all made the cut for Simpson. 

Unsurprisingly Salah, who has been a revelation at Anfield and tops the scoring charts, also received a vote. 

Elsewhere, recently capped England international James Tarkowski impressed Simpson enough to start at the back. 

David de Gea was the only Manchester United pick and Tottenham's Ben Davies also made the cut. 

Danny Simpson tweeted his votes for Team of the Year and voted Mo Salah the best player

The 25-year-old Liverpool forward is one of the favourites to win PFA Player of the Year

Salah and De Bruyne are front-runners to win Player of the Year this season. 

The Manchester City playmaker has been sensational for the champions elect with seven goals and 14 assists in the league. 

Salah has terrorised defences with his lightning pace and goalscoring threat, notching 29 goals and nine assists, enough for Simpson to deem him worthy of the prestigious award.
