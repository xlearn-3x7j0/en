Benjamin Mendy stars in promo video for Premier League stars barber

Benjamin Mendy has missed most of the season with injury but ahead of his return to the Manchester City first-team he has taken on his acting debut by starring in a promotional video for A Star Barbers. 

The Surrey-based barbers have grown renowned for cutting Premier League stars' hair since becoming popular with the Chelsea players.

Now they have produced a minute-long video where they hold nothing back as A Star Barbers owner, Ahmed Alsanawi, cuts Mendy's hair while the pair relax in luxury.

Benjamin Mendy stars in a promotional video for famous footballer barbers, A Star Barbers

In the video Mendy (right) gets his hair cut by Ahmed Alsanawi who owns A Star 

The video involves Mendy getting his haircut in a football room covered in words 'Let's Play'

A Star Barbers shot to fame after becoming the personal barber for Chelsea players at their Cobham training ground.

Alsanawi was subsequently banned when Chelsea accused him of exploiting the players and damaging their protected image rights through the use of selfies for promotion online.

A whole string of stars still visit the barbers based near to Chelsea's training ground though, including players from the Manchester clubs, Paul Pogba and Benjamin Mendy.

Clearly a huge fan, Mendy has used some of his enforced time away from the football pitch to help put together a lengthy promotional video for the barbers.

The video involves the pair playing football over a pool, video games and billiards, inside Mendy's luxurious house. As well as getting his hair trimmed. 

The video is produced by Visual Muse and at one minute long, pulls out all the stops

Mendy (left) is known to have used the barbers before, after to moving to England last summer

Mendy has recently returned to training with the Manchester City squad and is expected to come back into the first team within the next couple of weeks. 

The Frenchman has missed the majority of the season making just five appearances for City since his £52million move.

He was due to be back in time for the Manchester derby but with two important games over the next week, Guardiola may wait to ease him in. Whenever he returns, his hair will be ready.
