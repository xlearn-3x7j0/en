First image of WrestleMania 34 set as WWE prepare for ‘Show of Shows’

Here's something to whet wrestling fans' appetites - the first picture of the set for WrestleMania 34.

The image, leaked on social media, shows the famous squared circle beginning to take shape.

It was taken inside the Mercedes-Benz Superdome in New Orleans, where the 'Show of Shows' will take place this weekend.

Builders are busy constructing the enormous and incredibly detailed set ahead of the event billed as The Greatest Spectacle in Sports Entertainment.

On Saturday, April 8, it will host Roman Reigns challenging Brock Lesnar for the WWE Universal Championship.

In might even be the best WrestleMania in WWE history, with the returns of legends, debuts of megastars and potential surprises.

Among the highlights will be UFC icon Ronda Rousey making her wrestling debut with Kurt freaking Angle, in a battle with Triple H and Stephanie McMahon.

And for the first time in 12 years, there will be two women's singles matches taking place with championship gold up for grabs in both.
