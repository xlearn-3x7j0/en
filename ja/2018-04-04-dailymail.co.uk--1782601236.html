ON THE BOARD: Diageo appoints drugs firm Shire's chairman Susan Kilsby

Published: 16:58 EDT, 4 April 2018 | Updated: 19:29 EDT, 4 April 2018

Johnnie Walker and Guinness owner Diageo has appointed drugs maker Shire’s chairman Susan Kilsby as non-executive director and member of its audit, nomination and remuneration committees. 

She has spent most of her career as an investment banker with roles at Barclays’ first investment banking arm and Credit Suisse. 

She has been on the board of Shire since 2011 and chairman since 2014.

She also holds roles at BBA Aviation and Goldman Sachs.
