Royals-Tigers game postponed, rescheduled for April 20

The Royals‘ game against the Tigers scheduled for Wednesday afternoon has been postponed due to foul weather in Detroit.

The game will be made up April 20 as part of a day-night doubleheader. The game scheduled for 6:10 will remain, and the rescheduled game will be played at 12:10.

The Royals are 1-3 following their 1-0 win over the Tigers on Tuesday.
