How Cristiano Ronaldo spent his weekend away from Real Madrid action

Cristiano Ronaldo enjoyed his weekend away from Real Madrid action in the Spanish sun.

Portuguese superstar Ronaldo was handed Easter off, despite Real tackling Las Palmas, in a bid to keep him fully fit for their Champions League clash with Juventus.

The 33-year-old has played 35 times for Real this season, netting 37 times, along with six appearances for Portugal in a gruelling season.

And with the La Liga title all but handed to Barcelona, Zinedine Zidane is keen to look after Ronaldo in the knockout phases of the Champions League and World Cup coming up.

Real travel to the Juventus Stadium on Wednesday for the first leg of their European quarter-final.

Los Blancos did without their star man as they swatted aside the Gran Caneria side in a 3-0 thrashing - with Gareth Bale hitting a double either side of Karim Benzema's penalty.

Meanwhile, Ronaldo took his break from his 17 strikes in his last eight games for the Bernabeu side by having a quiet weekend.

Ronaldo posted a picture to Instagram, with his girlfriend Georgina Rodriguez, which saw the couple sitting at a table with two glasses of drink in a garden along with the caption "Happy Easter".

The pair appeared in good spirits as they dined alfresco, with a dog joining them.

Ronaldo and Rodriguez had their first child together last year when daughter Alana Martina was born in November.

The former Manchester United winger also has seven year old son Cristiano Jnr and a set of twins - who were delivered by a surrogate mother.
