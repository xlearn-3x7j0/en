BREAKING NEWS: 'Active shooter' reported at YouTube HQ in California 

Published: 16:14 EDT, 3 April 2018 | Updated: 16:20 EDT, 3 April 2018

YouTube employees have reported that an active shooter is on the premises of their San Bruno headquarters in California. 

Employee Vadim Lavrusik tweeted at around 1pm on Tuesday: 'Active shooter at YouTube HQ. Heard shots and saw people running while at my desk. Now barricaded inside a room with coworkers.' 

This is a breaking news story. More to follow. Lavrusik was evacuated 20 minutes later. 

Startling photographs from its expansive campus show employees leaving the building with their hands in the air.
