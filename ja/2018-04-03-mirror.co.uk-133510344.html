Marcelo names current Arsenal star in his ultimate XI

Real Madrid defender Marcelo has revealed the best XI he has ever played with, naming ex-Tottenham Hotspur man Luka Modric as the captain and also picking Mesut Ozil.

Star full-back Marcelo has played with some incredible talents in his 12 seasons at the Santiago Bernabeu, and could also choose from his international teammates with Brazil.

The first name on the left-back's team-sheet was midfielder Modric, with Marcelo saying: "Luka Modric as captain, that one is amazing."

The 29-year-old chose ex-Inter Milan and Queens Park Rangers stopper Julio Cesar as the best goalkeeper he has played with, ignoring Madrid legend Iker Casillas.

Three Brazilians make the four-man defence, with Sergio Ramos joining Dani Alves, Thiago Silva and Roberto Carlos at the back.

Madrid teammates Casemiro and Luka Modric are in midfield, with Arsenal midfielder Ozil and Madrid coach Zinedine Zidane joining them.

Even though Marcelo never played with French legend Zidane, the full-back said: "Wait, I did not play with Zizou (Zidane). But he is my coach. I can do it."

Portuguese legend Ronaldo is in attack, but Marcelo found it difficult to choose a partner for his club teammate.

He says "I have the worst doubt about the striker" before naming Paris Saint-Germain star Neymar as Ronaldo's partner.

Marcelo was speaking ahead of the huge Champions League clash between Juventus and Real Madrid tonight.

The quarter-final first leg will be played at Juventus' Allianz Stadium and is a re-match of last year's final.

Marcelo's team in full: Julio Cesar; Dani Alves, Thiago Silva, Sergio Ramos, Roberto Carlos; Casemiro, Modric, Ozil, Zidane; Cristiano Ronaldo, Neymar.
