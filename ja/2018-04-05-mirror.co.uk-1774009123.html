What channel is Arsenal v CSKA Moscow? All the details you need

Arsenal face their first European quarter-final for eight years as they take one last shot at glory for the season in the Europa League .

The Gunners are aiming to qualify for next seasons Champions League by winning UEFA's secondary club competition.

Arsene Wengers side must first get past CSKA Moscow , but they will be feeling confident after their 5-1 aggregate win over AC Milan in the last-16 stage.

However, the last time these two met was twice during the Champions League group stage in 2006, with Arsenal losing one and drawing the other 0-0.

Despite the Gunners main threat being Atletico Madrid, they shouldn’t dismiss Moscow after they edged out well-fancied Lyon on away goals in the previous round.

When and where is the game?

The game will be hosted at the Emirates Stadium and kick off is scheduled for 20:05 (GMT) on Thursday, April 5.

What channel is the game on?

The match will be live on BT Sport 2, with coverage starting from 19:30 (GMT). You can also follow all the build-up and action via the live Mirror football blog.
