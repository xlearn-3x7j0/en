Middlesbrough v Millwall, Fulham v Sunderland live on Sky Sports

Last Updated: 04/04/18 5:17pm

Fulham v Sunderland and Middlesbrough v Millwall will both be shown live on Sky Sports on the penultimate weekend of the Sky Bet Championship season.

There will likely be plenty at stake as promotion-chasing Fulham host relegation-threatened Sunderland on Friday, April 27 (7.45pm).

Middlesbrough then entertain Millwall on Saturday, April 28 (5.30pm), with both sides currently in the play-off mix.

Boro are sixth in the table, two places and a point above Millwall, who have been in brilliant form in 2018.

Watch both games live on Sky Sports Football.
