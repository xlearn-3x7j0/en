England win first medal at Commonwealth Games

England's Aimee Willmott claimed gold in the women's 400 metres individual medley to deny Scotland's Hannah Miley a third successive title.

Willmott finished in four minutes 34.90 seconds as Miley, who won gold at Delhi 2010 and Glasgow 2014, finished 0.26secs behind.

Bronze went to Australia's Blair Evans in 4:38.23.
