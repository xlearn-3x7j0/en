West Indies win toss, batting first vs Pakistan in 3rd T20

KARACHI, Pakistan (AP) West Indies won the toss and chose to bat first against Pakistan in the third and final Twenty20 on Tuesday.

Pakistan is favored to sweep the series after winning the first two games by 143 and 82 runs.

Top-ranked Pakistan rested its frontline fast bowlers Hasan Ali and Mohammad Amir. Left-arm fast bowler Shaheen Afridi will make his Twenty20 debut, and Usman Shinwari received his first start in the series.

The West Indies, which has been outclassed, will debut Jamaican batsman Andre McCarthy in place of Kesrick Williams. They are batting first for the first time in the series.
