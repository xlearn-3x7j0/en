Austin police report new explosion

A new explosion is reported in Austin, Texas, following a series of deadly blasts in recent days.

One person was injured in what is believed to be a package explosion in the south of the city, the fire department said.

Two people were killed in six similar blasts in the area in recent weeks.

President Donald Trump said the bombings were were carried out by "sick people", pledging justice for the victims.

On Tuesday evening, the Austin fire department said a man in his thirties received "potentially serious" injuries in the explosion at the Goodwill charity shop on Brodie Lane.

The department said its crews were working on the scene, urging the public to avoid the area.
