Lauren Goodger  

The TOWIE star released her OMG! Workout on Boxing Day 2015, after losing four stone and five dress sizes. 

She said a new system called bio-dynamics, that targets the large muscle groups in the body to burn fat fast, had helped her finally shed weight after years of trying. 

However, Lauren hasn't been able to maintain her size eight figure. 

Since releasing her DVD, her weight has fluctuated constantly and she has been open about her battles to slim down for good.

Most recently, she's lost a stone after completing a 21 day juice cleanse in Portugal.

Charlotte Crosby 

The reality star released a 2015 workout DVD which came after she dropped from a size 16 to a tiny size six to eight. 

Three Minute Belly Blitz became the fastest-selling fitness DVD ever.

While she has managed to maintain a slimmer figure, Charlotte's weight has gone up and down. 

In August 2017, she admitted to fans she was struggling to fit into her size eight clothes after 'eating c**p', and vowed to go on another fitness kick.

Nadia Sawalha 

In 2010, the Loose Women panellist released Fat To Fab after losing three-and-a-half stone and going from a size 18 to a 10 in four months.

But she's admitted that it wasn't plain sailing from there, saying: 'Then I fluctuated like we all do. Like I say show me a season, I'll show you a stone.'

In 2016, she said that since hitting the menopause she'd put on 14lbs and was finding it harder than ever to lose weight. 

'I do see it as a bit of a war, that I'm going to have to keep fighting for the rest of my life,' she said.

Natalie Cassidy 

The Eastenders star's Then & Now Workout weight loss and fitness DVD sold more than 100,000 copies in its first month of release in 2007.

However, she later admitted: 'It was all a bit of a farce. It was all wrong.'

The actress revealed that dropping from a size 16 to a 6 for the best selling release left her feeling desperately unhappy. 

And despite telling people her dramatic weight loss was due to working out, there was also a lot of extreme calorie counting behind the scenes.

She has since kept weight off, but has not maintained a size six figure.
