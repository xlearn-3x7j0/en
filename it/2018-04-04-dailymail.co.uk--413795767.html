Published: 06:44 EDT, 4 April 2018 | Updated: 08:48 EDT, 4 April 2018

Manchester United are set to launch a £26million bid for Napoli left back Faouzi Ghoulam as Jose Mourinho prepares to replace Luke Shaw.

The Algerian defender has caught the attention of Mourinho after establishing himself as one of the best full backs in Italy in recent years, according to RMC.

The 27-year-old even beat Leicester's Riyad Mahrez to win Algerian Footballer of the Year for 2017 despite suffering a cruciate ligament injury earlier this season.

Ghoulam is believed to have a release clause of £52million after signing a new contract with Napoli in December.

The defender has also been linked with a move to United's rivals Manchester City before suffering his injury.

Mourinho's interest in Ghoulam could spell the end for Shaw's time at Old Trafford who continues to be linked with a move away from the club.

The Red Devils have also been linked with a move for Tottenham's Danny Rose which would see Shaw most likely be involved in the deal.

Chelsea are also interested in rescuing the England defender from his Old Trafford nightmare once the transfer window reopens in the summer.
