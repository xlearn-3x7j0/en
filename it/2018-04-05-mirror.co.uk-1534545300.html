Insight and opinions on the game's hot topics on the Mirror Football podcast

The Mirror Football podcast is your essential weekly listen on all of the game's hot topics.

Released every Thursday, the show is the perfect way to get set for a weekend of football, with regular big name guests joining the Mirror's top reporters to give their opinion and insight.

The likes of Sir Geoff Hurst, Lee Dixon, Jimmy Floyd Hasselbaink, Luis Garcia and Christian Ziege have all joined the podcast in recent weeks, alongside John Cross, Darren Lewis and the rest of the Daily Mirror's best-connected journalists.

On this week's podcast (released Thursday March 29):

  • A look back at England's performance during the international break - and a realistic look into what is a successful World Cup.
  • Paul Pogba is reportedly unhappy at Manchester United, so we assess where it is going wrong for him.
  • It's a similar story for Dele Alli, who is finding himself sweating on his place in England's World Cup starting XI.
  • The curious case of Everton: After a disappointing season, why they could be the ones to watch next year.
  • Does the 'Anfield welcome' really make a difference on big European nights?
