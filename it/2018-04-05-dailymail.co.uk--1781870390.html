Zlatan Ibrahimovic keeps it casual as he dines in Beverly Hills

  • Zlatan Ibrahimovic was seen heading to a Beverly Hills restaurant on Wednesday
  • The 36-year-old frontman joined LA Galaxy from Manchester United last month
  • He scored twice on his debut to seal a 4-3 win over Los Angeles FC on Saturday
  • Famous United fan Rachel Riley joked on Twitter that Ibrahimovic was God

Published: 14:10 EDT, 5 April 2018 | Updated: 14:12 EDT, 5 April 2018

Zlatan Ibrahimovic looked very comfortable in his new surroundings as he was pictured heading out for dinner in Beverly Hills on Wednesday night.

The former Manchester United striker has been an LA Galaxy player for just under a fortnight but he's already made a big impact on the pitch, scoring twice in a 20-minute cameo on debut to seal a dramatic 4-3 win over neighbours Los Angeles FC.

His trip out on Wednesday was much more understated as he wore jeans and a black tee-shirt under a long, thin coat.

Despite admitting he felt tired after his Galaxy bow, the 36-year-old could be in line to start for the MLS side when they take on Kansas City next Monday.

Ibrahimovic only played seven times for United this season, with his last appearance coming on Boxing Day.

Although his United career finished with him on the fringes of the first-team, Ibrahimovic remains a very popular figure at Old Trafford.

Famous United fan Rachel Riley, who is best known for her work on Channel 4's Countdown, called Ibrahimovic 'God' on Thursday.

Replying to a tweet that asked: 'You believe in God?', she wrote: 'Yep of course. Great debut for LA Galaxy on the weekend.'
