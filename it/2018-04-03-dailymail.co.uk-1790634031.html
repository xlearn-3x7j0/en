Lewis Hamilton shows off his tattoos in Bahrain before F1 Grand Prix

  • Lewis Hamilton was quick to show off his tattoos after arriving in Bahrain
  • Bahrain will host the second Grand Prix of the Formula One season this weekend
  • Rival Sebastian Vettel claimed they have no issue with each other

Published: 19:04 EDT, 3 April 2018 | Updated: 19:04 EDT, 3 April 2018

Lewis Hamilton took to showing off his tattoos on arriving in Bahrain as the Formula One season continues this weekend.

The four-time World Champion graced his six million Instagram followers with an image of the tattoos on his torso, along with his new hair.

Hamilton will get to work straight away as he looks to bring home his first win of the season, after Sebastian Vettel snatched victory away from him in Australia.

Lewis Hamilton shows off his tattoos and new hairstyle in lavish Bahrain hotel bathroom

Hamilton also shared a video of the sun setting over Bahrain after he arrived on Tuesday

The Brit also posted a video of the sun setting over Bahrain as he made his way to his hotel ahead of another week of preparations himself and the Mercedes team.

Hamilton will be looking to overturn the early points deficit to Vettel this weekend after a safety car disrupted his race and handed the German the victory.

As last years rivarly looks set to reignite, the Ferrari driver squashed rumours that the pair do not get along by claiming they 'don't have a problem with each other.' 

''It's fine as long as we joke with each other and I think even if we are very different persons, I think we share - all of us - a common passion and that makes us quite equal again,' Vettel said.

'We love racing and we try to do our best, once we're in the car and beat all the others.

'I think in that regard, I don't see why we shouldn't get on with each other.'
