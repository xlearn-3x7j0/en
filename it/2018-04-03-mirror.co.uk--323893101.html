Tottenham to 'fight it out with Arsenal' for £50m rated defender

Tottenham are ready to go head to head with north London rivals Arsenal in a bid to snatch Dutch wonderkid Matthijs De Ligt.

Spurs want Ajax's teen ace De Ligt to replace Toby Alderweireld - with Manchester United hovering to claim the Belgian centre back, according to football.london .

Whoever grabs him will have to splash around £50million to lure him from Amsterdam.

The Gunners have already jumped the gun in the race for the 18-year-old's signature after sending scouts to Holland to watch De Ligt in action during a friendly against England.

De Ligt became Ajax's youngest ever captain in January and has also attracted the attention of Barcelona following a series of eye-catching performances.

OTHER NEWS

West Ham want to keep David Moyes beyond this season. The Hammers, set to hold their annual review in the summer, already believe Moyes is the man for the long term after his work at the London Stadium since being hired in November. The Scot, who is on a contract until the end of the current campaign, has taken West Ham out of the drop zone and up to 14th — five points clear of the dreaded dotted line. Tellingly, Moyes has already scouted potential summer transfer targets.

Rafa Benitez is wanted by Real Sociedad. The Basque club, currently just 15th in Spain's 20-team top flight but safe from relegation, hope to convince the Newcastle manager to come home and lead them up the table.

Accrington Stanley owner Andy Holt has hit out at the Football League after they demanded an explanation over the club's burger-based bonus scheme. When the League Two side win, Holt treats the players to a post-match takeaway. If they lose, they have to buy their own.
