Former teammate has perfect suggestion for what Ronaldo should do next

Plenty were left lost for words when Cristiano Ronaldo's incredible overhead kick found the back of the Juventus net on Tuesday night.

But Alvaro Arbeloa wasn't one of them.

The former Real Madrid and Spain right-back played with Ronaldo at the Bernabeu for seven years, rejoining the club he played for as a youngster in 2009 after two-and-a-half years at Liverpool.

The World Cup and double European Championship winner ended his career at West Ham last season, and he seemed to suggest that Ronaldo should now do the same after such an incredible goal - the type of which simply can't be bettered on earth.

" Cristiano Ronaldo can now leave the Earth and play against the Martians," Arbeloa tweeted. "He's done everything here!"

And you know what, he's probably right.

Ronaldo's goal - his second strike of the evening - helped Real on the way to a 3-0 first leg win, with Marcelo helping himself to the third.

The Portuguese has now scored 119 times in the Champions League, with his 37th strike of the season in all competitions drawing him level with Mohamed Salah as the top scorer in Europe's top divisions this season.
