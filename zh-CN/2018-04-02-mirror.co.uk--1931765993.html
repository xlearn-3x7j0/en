Huge boost for Manchester United and Barcelona in Alderweireld chase

Toby Alderweireld fears he is being frozen out of Spurs after failing to agree a new contract.

The defender is understood to have been axed from the squad for Sunday’s win over Chelsea without an explanation.

The move came despite Belgium boss Roberto Martinez warned Alderweireld that his place in Russia will be at stake if the 29-year-old does not get more game time.

Alderweireld is being chased by Chelsea, Manchester United and Barcelona with Spurs refusing to meet his demands for a new deal worth £180,000-a-week.
