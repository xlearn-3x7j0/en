WWE Raw: Ronda Rousey to confront Stephanie McMahon as WrestleMania looms

Ronda Rousey and Kurt Angle will confront Stephanie McMahon and Triple H tonight on the last WWE Raw before WrestleMania 34 .

The UFC icon and the Olympic Gold Medallist will meet WWE's chief brand officer and The Game in the ring ahead of their tag team clash.

Rousey threw HHH through a table at Elimination Chamber after Angle revealed he and Stephanie were trying to manipulate her.

Triple H later responded by sucker punching Kurt, but that earned his wife a Samoan Drop from Ronda and him an Angle ankle lock.

What do WWE's power couple have in store for Rousey and Raw's general manager before their showdown in New Orleans on April 8?

The broadcast from the Philips Arena in Atlanta, Georgia is also the penultimate chance for The Undertaker to respond to John Cena.

Cena has become increasingly desperate in his bid to lure Taker out for a match at WrestleMania and has hurled insults at The Deadman.

Last week on Raw he bested The Phenom's brother Kane in a no-disqualification match and he has even taken to goading Taker on Twitter.

It's not clear whether The Demon of Death Valley pays a great deal of attention to social media, but he will be well aware of Cena's taunts.

Unless he makes an appearance on SmackDown LIVE tomorrow evening, Big Evil must respond to the 16-time world champion tonight.

Seth Rollins and Finn Balor will go head-to-head in singles competition just days before they challenge for the Intercontinental Championship.

The pair of former world champions are set to take part in a triple threat match with The Miz on the Grandest Stage of Them All on Sunday.

But first the Kingslayer will get the opportunity to earn a psychological advantage by defeating the Irishman who until now has had his number.

After Nia Jax bested Alexa Bliss’ ally Mickie James, the calculating Raw Women’s Champion tried to cheap shot her former best friend.

Can the Goddess survive a beatdown from The Irresistible Force as she looks to make her pay for her cruel bullying and snide betrayal?
