Liverpool star set for season-ending surgery

Liverpool defender Joel Matip is set to miss the rest of the season with a thigh injury.

The Reds have confirmed Matip is 'likely' to need surgery on the problem which was sustained in the 2-1 win at Crystal Palace on Saturday.

Centre-half Matip got through 90 minutes at Selhurst Park but medical assessment later revealed the issue, with surgery expected to be required.

If he does go under the knife, it will end his involvement in the remainder of the 2017-18 campaign.

Liverpool boss Jurgen Klopp will provide a further update on Tuesday afternoon when he speaks to the press ahead of the Reds' Champions League quarter-final first leg against Manchester City at Anfield.

The news comes as a blow to Liverpool with Matip having featured 31 times in all competitions so far this term.

Klopp will also address the prognosis on Adam Lallana after the England midfielder was forced off shortly after entering the action as a substitute in the second half at Palace.
