Grilled Turkey Tenderloins Recipe

The tasty marinade of this recipe makes tenderloins of turkey even more tender and delicious, especially when grilled. A quick and easy main dish for the busy cook.

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup sherry cooking wine
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons onion, chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon ginger, powdered
  • 4 turkey tenderloins
  • pepper to taste
  • dash of seasoning salt

Container: large resealable plastic bag

  • Mix all ingredients in large plastic bag. Add turkey and turn bag to coat.
  • Marinade in refrigerator overnight.
  • Grill 6-8 minutes per side.
