Arsenal fans spot what Ozil said to Bellerin in heated on-pitch argument

It was all fine for Arsenal in the end against Stoke City on Sunday, but for a while it looked as though it wouldn't be.

The Gunners laboured for much of the game against the relegation-threatened Potters, finally making the breakthrough when Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang slotted home from the penalty spot after Bruno Martins-Indi had fouled Mesut Ozil in the box.

Aubameyang would add another goal and then selflessly pass over penalty duty to Alexandre Lacazette for Arsenal's third in stoppage time, but it was clear that nerves were frayed while the score stayed at 1-0.

Because just before Aubameyang's second goal and with the game a little fraught, Gunners supporters spotted an exchange between Ozil and Hector Bellerin which was, well, quite funny in truth.

The German was clearly upset that Bellerin didn't release a pass earlier, meaning he was offside when he did, and was then seen seemingly saying to his teammate: "You talk too much, wait until inside the dressing room."

At least that's what Arsenal fans on social media thought.

But it seems as though all was well that ended well for the Arsenal pair, with Bellerin tweeting his shocked face at full-time with the words "When Mesut Ozil walks into the dressing room... love you bro".

No-one can stay angry at Ozil for very long, it seems.
