Sir Mick Jagger, 74, dances in funny video from Bequia music festival

Sir Mick Jagger is keen to prove he still has the moves at 74. 

The great-grandad showed off his best shape-throwing skills in a social media video on Monday, as he partied at a music festival in the Caribbean island of Bequia.

Clad in a summery blue shirt and cap, the Rolling Stones rocker proved to be as lively as ever as he danced around on the beach, beside numerous other party-goers.
