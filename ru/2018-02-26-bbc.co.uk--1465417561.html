Four people have died in an explosion at a shop in Leicester on Sunday night, police have confirmed.

Emergency services responded to reports of an explosion and fire at a building in Hinckley Road at about 19:00 GMT.

A search and rescue operation was still under way this morning as police search for clues into the cause of the blast.
