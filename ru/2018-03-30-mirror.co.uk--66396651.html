Liverpool press conference live as Klopp provides Gomez update

Jurgen Klopp will speak from Melwood on Friday afternoon ahead of his side's crunch clash against Crystal Palace at Selhurst Park.

Liverpool currently sit third in the Premier League and will be looking to cement their place in the top four, especially with Chelsea and Spurs facing off on Sunday.

However for Klopp one eye will be on the huge Champions League quarter-final first leg against Manchester City on Tuesday.

While the German can't afford for his side to drop points in the league, he will be keen to field a full strength side at Anfield on Tuesday.

One player who will be missing from Liverpool's squad on Saturday is Joe Gomez. The key defender suffered an ankle injury in the first 10 minutes of England's clash against Netherlands.

Jurgen Klopp admits to "initial shock" over Joe Gomez injury

Liverpool manager Jurgen Klopp has revealed that Joe Gomez’s ankle injury picked up on international duty isn’t as bad as was first feared.

The Reds defender was forced off after just 10 minutes of England’s friendly with Holland last week in Amsterdam , and there were initial suggestions that he could be out for the remainder of the season, putting his World Cup hopes in jeopardy.

But Klopp now says that isn’t the case, but he has ruled the 20-year-old out of “the next couple of games”.
