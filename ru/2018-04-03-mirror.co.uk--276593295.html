Shane McMahon's official WWE salary has been revealed

If you thought footballers were well paid, then wrestlers certainly give them a run for their money.

The WWE this week submitted paperwork and reports to the Securities and Exchange Commission - a requirement for their April shareholders meeting.

The 40-page document can be read in full here , but included are the 2017 salaries and total compensation amounts for their executive team.

These include those of Chairman and CEO Vince McMahon, Executive Vice President, Talent, Live Events & Creative Paul “Triple H” Levesque as well as his wife Stephanie and her brother Shane.

Unsurprisingly, Mr McMahon, who ranked 1394th in Forbes' list of billionaires with a net worth of $1.8billion, earned the most on his salary, pocketing a cool $1.4million with his overall pay eventually reaching $3million.

Triple H's salary, however, doesn't even compare as he earned just $650,000, but it's worth noting that his overall paycheque, no thanks to 'performance fees and royalties', was an impressive $3.2million - more than the CEO.

Relatives of the CEO are listed slightly differently as they're not independent contractors - but his offspring still pocketed hefty sums.

Stephanie ended the year with $2.2million for her role as Chief Brand Officer whilst Shane was paid $1.3million for performances on SmackDown and PPV.
