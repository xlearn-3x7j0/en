No-one can believe what Cristiano Ronaldo did against Juventus

Wow.

There have been many, many times over the years when Cristiano Ronaldo has done things to take people's breath away, but this one might just top the lot.

On Tuesday night, the Real Madrid forward produced arguably his best goal of a long, successful career when he quite incredibly launched into a overhead kick from a Dani Carvajal cross .

The ball initially seemed too high for him to make a strong enough contact with the ball, but Ronaldo doesn't seem to deal in conventional physics any more, and his effort flew into the corner of the net past a helpless Gianluigi Buffon.

On Twitter, people - including ex-England forward Gary Lineker - couldn't quite believe it.

Has he ever scored a better one?
