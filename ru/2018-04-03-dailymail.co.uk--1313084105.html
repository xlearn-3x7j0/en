Taylor Swift's accused stalker sentenced to 10 years probation

Published: 18:58 EDT, 2 April 2018 | Updated: 20:30 EDT, 2 April 2018

Taylor Swift's accused stalker Frank Andrew Hoover was reportedly sentenced to 10 years probation on Monday for violating a protective order back in 2016.

Hoover must remain a mile away from the 28-year-old pop star and her family, whom he allegedly sent threatening emails to (including her father Scott) - according to TMZ.

The convicted felon will avoid prison time if he undergoes psychological testing, substance abuse classes, random drug testing, and surrenders all firearms.
