How Tottenham plan to keep keep Pochettino away from Real Madrid

Mauricio Pochettino is set to be handed a bumper new deal by Tottenham to fend of Real Madrid, according to the Telegraph.

He will reportedly be the first of seven contracts which Spurs look to renew in the coming months.

The Telegraph report that Pochettino will join Dele Alli, Harry Kane, Jan Vertonghen, Hugo Lloris, Son Heung-min and Christian Eriksen in being offered huge deals.

Pochettino’s reported offer would make him become one of the best-paid managers in the Premier League.

It is thought that the deal would be worth around £8.5million-a-year - and be enough to fend off Real’s interest.
