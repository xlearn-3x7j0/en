Liverpool lose Mohamed Salah to injury during Champions League tie against Manchester City

Liverpool forward Mohamed Salah was forced off with an injury in the second half of their Champions League clash with Manchester City.

The Egyptian got the opener as a rampant Liverpool side raced into a 3-0 half-time lead but he went off in the 52nd minute to be replaced by Georginio Wijnaldum, after suffering an apparent muscle injury.
