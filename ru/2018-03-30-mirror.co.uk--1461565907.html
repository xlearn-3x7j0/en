Anthony Joshua and Joseph Parker weight revealed ahead of heavyweight fight

Anthony Joshua has weighed in at 242lb (17st 4lb) for his heavyweight unification clash with Joseph Parker.

The IBF and WBA world champion had been expected to weigh far less than he did for his win over Carlos Takam last October.

And he did just that coming in almost a stone lighter than he did prior to stopping Takam in the 11th round.

Parker, meanwhile, tipped the scales at 236lb (16st 12lb), 9lb lighter than he was when he faced Hughie Fury last September.

Joshua said: "I feel better. Training is all about rehearsal and so is sparring but it has gone well

"You will see I will be a lot sharper.

"My reflexes will be sharper and my speed will be much sharper.

"He looked good, but I would expect that from a world champion.

"He is nimble, quick and has boxing skills but I can control him and match him for speed, I thinjkl.

"I will be going in and out, trying to control it. you will see. I am looking forward to it."

"You will be in for a good, good fight tomorrow night, believe me."

Parker will put his WBO belt on the line at the Principality Stadium in Cardiff as both men bid to take another step towards becoming the undisputed champion.

And he said: "Us being close in weight makes me think he is taking it seriously.

"We are similar, we will both be fast tomorrow and throwing bombs.

"I saw he is confident, I'm expecting a really good fight.

"The first few rounds could go either way, depends who follows their game plan better - I'm ready for anything.

"I'm here for war, you'll have to do everything to beat me."
