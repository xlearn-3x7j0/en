Viewers slam McManaman's commentary for one very bizarre reason

Viewers have taken to Twitter to hit out at Steve McManaman's commentary of Liverpool's 3-0 win over Manchester City for one very specific reason.

The BT Sport pundit was alongside Darren Fletcher and Glenn Hoddle in the Anfield gantry as the hosts produced an emphatic performance.

First half goals from Mohamed Salah, Alex Oxlade-Chamberlain and Sadio Mane won the tie early on.

But McManaman, who played for both Liverpool and Manchester City, annoyed fans for the way he called out Vincent Kompany's name.

The 46-year-old kept referring to the Belgian centre-back as 'Vinny Kompany'.
