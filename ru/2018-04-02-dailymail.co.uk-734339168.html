Kaley Cuoco reveals she dumped a famous ex because he was a bad tipper

Kaley Cuoco has revealed that she once dumped a famous boyfriend because he was 'cheap' - and an incredibly bad tipper.

The 32-year-old aired her dirty laundry to Vogue in a new video in which she describes her past relationship with a well-known celebrity who was so cheap he wouldn't leave tips for any servers.

Keeping the real man's name anonymous, the Big Bang Theory star chose to call him John throughout the video to protect his privacy and admitted to dating him when she was just 19-years-old and just starting her career.

Kaley shared with the magazine that before she was a celebrity she was a, 'Sweet wide-eyed 19-year-old,' who was just starting out her career. 

'I was just starting to make a little money and starting out when I met a guy - a hot guy, she said of her boyfriend at the time whose name she refused to disclose. 

However, she did share that he's an actor and confessed, 'Let's call him John because if I told you his name, you'd know who he is.'

Explaining that the couple's relationship was running smoothly, everything went terribly wrong when she found out he is a horrible tipper.

Not only was he a bad tipper to servers at restaurants, drivers or with anyone that would help them while they were out, she said he was just straight cheap.

When they went on their first official date at a fancy restaurant, she knew things were bad. 

Gushing about her old beau she said, 'He was magical. Everyone that met him loved him too and we looked cute together - nothing could go wrong,' until it did.

'He wanted to take me on a really nice romantic dinner date. It was kind of a surprise and I was so excited because we hadn't really done this yet.

'We had this great dinner, we got along and the conversation was great,' she shared. 

Kaley even offered to split the bill with him but he was insistent on paying but when the bill finally came he started looking at it seriously while squinting before closing the bill back up without putting a card down or paying.

He then took his phone out and began multiplying an eight per cent tip which is when she said a light-bulb went off and she realized, 'Oh my god. He's cheap. He's a bad tipper,' she said with shock.

She then backtracked and gave a timeline of what happened in their relationship saying, 'We start dating and going on really adorable dates where we took my dogs on walks and watch movies.

Although Kaley didn't reveal who John really is, taking a look back at all of her exes you may get some idea.

Then in 2004 when she was 19-years old, the age Kaley admitted to dating the mystery man, she was in a relationship with Thad Luckinbill, 42, from the CBS soap opera The Young and the Restless.

By 2005 at the age of 19 and 20, she was hot and heavy with singer, Jaron Lowenstein, 44, before the two broke up.

In 2008, she dated her Big Bag Theory co-star Johnny Galecki, with their relationship going on to last for two years. However while Kaley refused to name the man she was referring to in the Vogue video, she did speak out to defend Johnny, insisting in an Instagram Story video that he is 'a great tipper'.  

Whoever the mystery man is, in the video Kaley recalls feeling like they would be together forever, 'I'm going to marry him, this is the father of my children I'm so in love!' she joked.

However, once the tipping situation occurred she was having second thoughts about everything. 

She describes that she grew up with great parents and despite not being rich, her father would always tip people which she learned to do in her own life. 

'I knew I had to be with a guy in the future who was the same because I was that way,' she admitted.

So when she saw John act horribly at the restaurant she recalled thinking to herself, 'This can't be. How would I ever introduce this guy to my dad?' 

Not being generous wasn't the official deal-breaker for Kaley though and she admits that a part of her was thinking she, 'Can't just break up with a guy because he's cheap.'

Their relationship started to get a little weird when he started asking her to go on dates but she would suggest staying in instead which led him to start thinking she was an introvert.   

When they would go out she said she would make up lies like, 'Oh baby I left my sunglasses inside,' before running back inside to tip people.  

'I just didn't know how we could progress and I think he started to think I was a little bit crazy too and losing my mind. 

'He's thinking I'm crazy when I'm trying to cover his cheap tracks! People thought he was so generous but I was going broke,' she joked.

Everything ended when John met her family for the first time and took them out to dinner, grabbing the check at the end saying he'll pay. 

'Oh my god yea I bet you're f*****g taking everyone out tonight. $600 bill you're going to leave $13 - I was horrified,' Kaley raved.

'I can't let my dad who my dad is like my idol, see my cheap boyfriend. That's what it was. My cheap, hot boyfriend and my dad doesn't care if he's hot, my dad cares if he tips.

'I couldn't ever admit to my dad that that's who this guy was. I wouldn't have been able to live with myself, no matter how hot he was,' she confessed. 

After dinner Kaley ran back inside, tipped the server, never went back to the restaurant again and broke up with the boyfriend a week later. 

At the very end of the video Kaley looks directly into the camera and says, 'You would die if I told you who this was. Okay, I'm going to tell you. His name is -' and then the video cuts off.

Despite the fact that she had a horrible ex-boyfriend, Kaley was lucky enough to meet her current fiance Karl Cook, 26, who is a professional equestrian.

However, before finding her Prince Charming she dated a handful of men in between but none of them could be John because she said she was 19-years-young and at the start of her career when she dated him.

Some of her past boyfriends include musician Christopher French, 35, who she dated in January 2011 and by October was engaged to addiction specialist Josh Resnik, 44, after calling it quits five months later.

By 2012 Kaley moved on and was dating musician Bret Bollinger, 38, before dating Henry Cavill, 34, in 2013 for just 10 days.

After her stint with Henry, Kaley married Ryan Sweeting, 30, in December 2013 but the pair divorced in 2016 when she broke up with him for being an addict .
