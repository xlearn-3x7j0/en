Since the dawn of time, parents have been put on this earth for one purpose; to embarrass the hell out of their children.  

Footballers know this only too well. Take Eden Hazard's tricky time explaining his dad's comments surrounding his contract at Chelsea, or imagine being a 26-year-old Tom Ince still being promised pocket money from your old man for beating Manchester United in the FA Cup. There is no escape. 

So it really should have come as no surprise to Sergio Aguero that when he headed out in Cheshire with his father Leonel del Castillo that this would be no usual evening stroll when the cameras spotted the pair.

Sergio Aguero looked just a tad uneasy as his dad Leonel del Castillo played up for the cameras

The expression on Aguero's old man's face suggested he was up for some mishchief

Leonel's immediate expression for the cameras suggested he was up to know good, and Aguero's uneasy expression suggests as much.

But the Argentine soon saw the funny side as his father hammed it up for the snaps, and was happy to pose too. 

There is no doubting Leonel's massive amount of pride in his son, and the Manchester City striker has every reason to wear a smile at the moment. 

The 29-year-old is enjoying a phneomenal season at the Etihad, having netted 21 times in 22 league appearances this season.

Pep Guardiola's side are still on course for the quadruple too, and face Wigan in the FA Cup fifth round on Monday night.
