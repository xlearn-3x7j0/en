Bubble hotel on tiny Martinique island has transparent walls 

If you're looking to immerse yourself in nature, then an unusual hotel on a remote island in the Caribbean aims to tick that box.

Le Domaine des Bulles, which is situated on Île Petite Grenade off Martinique, features four bedrooms housed in bubble-like sleeping pods.

The spacious domed structures are transparent, allowing guests to gaze at the stars or leafy surroundings from the comfort of their beds.

While some holidaymakers might be worried about their privacy, each bedroom is surrounded by a private garden.

And in the true spirit of glamping, each bubble is decked out with WiFi, a coffee machine and mini bar.

There is also an air conditioning unit to prevent the shelters from overheating in the tropical sun. In the private grounds, there are bubbling hot tubs and sheltered deck areas.   

Elsewhere on the site, there is a spa offering massages and a restaurant serving up fresh seafood.

Each of the rooms can sleep up to three guests and rates start from £117 a night.  

To date, Le Domaine des Bulles has got an 'excellent' rating on TripAdvisor with a total of 32 reviews. 

One patron, LoveParisMetz, described it as a 'haven of peace' and said she was lucky to see a 'magic starry night'.

Meanwhile AmelieKite said 'everything was perfect' and the 'experience was magical'. Although she added that the signpost on the road was a little difficult to spot.
