Shannon Sharpe reacts to Raiders coach Jon Gruden cutting punter Marquette King over ‘personality’ issues (VIDEO)

Apr 2, 2018 at 10:29a ET

In a conversation with Skip Bayless on Undisputed, Shannon Sharpe disagrees with new Oakland Raiders head coach Jon Gruden's decision to cut punter Marquette King, who ranked third among punters in net average last season. Shannon: "I don't like anything that the Raiders have done this offseason."
