Cardinals-Brewers game Wednesday airs on FS1

The Cardinals’ game on Wednesday, April 24, at Milwaukee will be televised nationally on FS1. It’s the Cardinals’ first appearance on FS1 this season.

Pregame coverage starts at 6 p.m. CT, with the game at 6:30 p.m. The game will also stream on FOX Sports GO.
