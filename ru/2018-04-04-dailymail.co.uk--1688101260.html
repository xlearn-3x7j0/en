Monaco's Fabinho interested in joining Manchester United this summer

  • Monaco midfielder Fabinho eager to join Manchester United this summer
  • The Brazilian is valued at £55million and is very highly sought after 
  • United could overhaul their midfield with Marouane Fellaini set to depart

Published: 12:31 EDT, 4 April 2018 | Updated: 12:32 EDT, 4 April 2018

Monaco midfielder Fabinho wants to join Manchester United this summer as he seeks to test himself in the Premier League.

The 24-year-old is one of the standout defensive midfielders in world football and is highly sought after, with it being anticipated that it would take around £55million to prise him away from Monaco.

As reported by The Sun, United is Fabinho's preferred destination and the move could materialise.

Monaco midfielder Fabinho, who is valued at £55million, wants to join Manchester United

FABINHO'S CAREER 

2012-2015 - Rio Ave

2012-2013 - Real Madrid (loan)

2013-2015 - Monaco (loan)

2015- present - Monaco 

United manager Jose Mourinho is likely to make significant alterations to his squad this summer as he seeks to ensure that his side can compete with Manchester City and challenge for the Premier League title next campaign.

Marouane Fellaini is likely to leave United while another midfielder, Michael Carrick, is retiring at the conclusion of the campaign.

Fabinho previously worked under Mourinho, albeit fleetingly, when he was a youngster at Real Madrid, with the Portuguese giving the Brazilian a runout against Malaga in La Liga in May 2013.

United manager Jose Mourinho is eager to overhaul his midfield options this summer

Since joining Monaco, initially on loan, Fabinho has developed significantly and his ability to win the ball back and play short, snappy passes to his team-mates makes him an attractive asset.

In addition to Fabinho, United have also been linked with moves for Jean Michael-Seri of Nice and Fred of Shakhtar Donetsk.
