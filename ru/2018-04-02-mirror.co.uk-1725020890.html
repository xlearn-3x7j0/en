WWE WrestleMania Axxess tournaments to offer NXT title opportunities

WWE has announced four invitational tournaments taking place at WrestleMania Axxess giving the winners NXT title opportunities.

For the first time, the fan convention will offer performers the chance to earn championship matches - just hours before WrestleMania 34 .

The tournaments are for the WWE UK Championship, the NXT North American title, the NXT Tag Team titles and the NXT Women's title.

They will feature WWE UK talents including Mark Andrews and reported new signing Zack Gibson, who are bidding to face WWE UK Champion Pete Dunne.

Meanwhile former WWE UK Champion Tyler Bate and Trent Seven, aka Moustache Mountain, will battle to face the NXT Tag Team Champions.

If that wasn't enough, some of the top stars from London's groundbreaking PROGRESS Wrestling will also be wowing fans in New Orleans.

The contests will begin on Thursday on Axxess Day 1 and culminate on Axxess Day 4 on Sunday, with the winners facing the respective champions.

It means WWE UK Champion Pete Dunne will defend his title against the WWE UK invitational winner - which could be 'Liverpool's Number One' Gibson.

The acclaimed 26-year-old, from Maghull, Merseyside, has been paired in the first round with Welsh high flying wizard Mark Andrews, 26, from Cardiff.

Meanwhile, the new NXT North American Champion - to be crowned at NXT TakeOver: New Orleans on Saturday - will make his first ever title defence.

Axxess will feature competitors from WWE NXT, WWE 205 Live, the UK Championship Tournament, the Mae Young Classic, PROGRESS and EVOLVE Wrestling.

Standout names set for action include the Austrian behemoth Walter, from PROGRESS, who will clash with NXT’s Kassius Ohno in an international dream match.

Currently, the action for the four days of WrestleMania Axxess has been scheduled as follows:

Axxess Day 1 - Thursday, 6pm

The WWE UK Championship Invitational First Round

Lio Rush vs. Buddy Murphy

Dan Matha vs. Drew Gulak

NXT North American Invitational First Round

Fabian Aichner vs. Jason Kincaid

Akira Tozawa vs. Kona Reeves

NXT Tag Team Invitational First Round

Moustache Mountain vs. Tino Sabbatelli & Riddick Moss

Street Profits vs. Cezar Bononi & Adrian Jaoude

NXT Women’s Invitational First Round

Nikki Cross vs. Aliyah

Kairi Sane vs. Bianca Belair

In addition, the session will feature a WWN Championship Match between titleholder Keith Lee and NXT’s Kassius Ohno as well as action featuring 205 Live Superstars TJP, Kalisto, Gentleman Jack Gallagher and Buddy Murphy, superstars from the Mae Young Classic and more.

Axxess Day 2 - Friday, 5pm

WWE U.K. Championship Invitational First Round

Mark Andrews vs. Zack Gibson

NXT North American Invitational First Round

Hideo Itami vs. Wolfgang

NXT Tag Team Invitational First Round

SAnitY vs. Forgotten Sons

TM61 vs. Oney Lorcan & Danny Burch

NXT Women’s Invitational First Round

Dakota Kai vs. Lacey Evans

Candice LeRae vs. Peyton Royce

In addition, the session will include matches featuring superstars from NXT, the Mae Young Classic, PROGRESS and more.

Axxess Day 3, Session 1 - Saturday, 8am

This will include semi-final matches from the four invitationals. In addition, it will include matches featuring superstars from NXT, the WWE UK Championship Tournament, the Mae Young Classic and more.

Axxess Day 3, Session 2 - Saturday, 1pm

This will include the NXT North American Invitation Final, a WWE UK Championship Invitational semi-final, an EVOLVE Tag Team Championship Match, as well as matches featuring superstars from 205 Live, NXT, the Mae Young Classic and more.

Axxess Day 3, Session 3 - Saturday, 6pm

This will include the WWE UK Championship Invitational Final, the NXT Tag Team Invitational Final and the NXT Women’s Invitational Final, as well as NXT’s Kassius Ohno vs. PROGRESS star Walter.

In addition, it will include matches featuring 205 Live’s TJP, Kalisto and Gran Metalik, superstars from NXT and the Mae Young Classic and more.

Axxess Day 4 - Sunday, 8am

Day 4 will include the following championship matches:

WWE U.K. Champion Pete Dunne vs. WWE UK Championship Invitational winner

NXT North American Champion vs. NXT North American Invitational winner (First-ever title defence)

NXT Tag Team Champions vs. NXT Tag Team Invitational winners

In addition, it will feature a WWN Championship Match, a PROGRESS Women’s Championship Match, a PROGRESS Championship Match, a PROGRESS Tag Team Championship match and more.
