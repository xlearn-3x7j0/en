Grilled Cheese Pie Recipe

Hereâ€™s a blast from my past, mom used to make this 20 years ago. Pretty simple and tastes good.

  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • salt and pepper
  • oregano, to taste
  • 1 cup muenster cheese, diced
  • 1 cup ham, chopped

Mix egg, flour, milk, salt, pepper and oregano to taste. Stir in cheese and ham. Bake in a well greased pie pan for 40 minutes at 425 degrees.
