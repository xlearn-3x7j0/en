Barcelona host Roma in the quarter finals of the Champions League on Wednesday night. 

Barcelona are the big favourites to gain an advantage in the first leg of their tie but Roma will be hopeful after losing just once in their last eight games in all competitions.

Ernesto Valverde's side have reached the last eight of the Champions League for the last 11 seasons. Roma are in the quarter finals for the first time since 2008.
