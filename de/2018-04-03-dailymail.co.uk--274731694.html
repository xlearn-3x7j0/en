Morbidly obese pooch sheds almost 25lbs in four months

A morbidly obese dog has shed almost 25 pounds in four months after being put on a strict diet to curb her desire for deli meat.

Eight-year-old Snuffy from Virginia, USA, was three times the size of a normal Beagle, gobbling down huge meals and snacking on sliced ham and turkey.

The podgy pup, whose elderly owners couldn't resist treating her to decadent meals, struggled to scratch her ears without panting and had a deafening snore.

Her weight crept up to 64.5 pounds - three times the weight of a healthy Beagle, which is between 22 and 24 pounds.

After Snuffy's owners moved away she was adopted by 26-year-old Caroline Webster, who has been caring for the pooch since November 2017.

Caroline, a vet's assistant in the town of Herndon in Virginia, decided her new porky pet needed an intervention.

She put Snuffy on a strict dog food diet of two meals a day, cut out her indulgent deli meat and walked her for three miles every day.

Snuffy after her dramatic weight loss. She can now run around easily for the first time in years

The pooch was even frogmarched up and down the stairs to her third-floor apartment during the drastic new regime.

And the hard work paid off.

Snuffy lost an incredible 24.5 pounds in just four months and is on track to hit her 30lb goal weight just in time for the summer. 

Caroline Webster with Snuffy. She adopted the porky pooch and decided to get her new pet's health back on track

Snuffy enjoying a walk in the sunshine. Caroline walked her for three miles every day to help her shed the pounds

Caroline said Snuffy is a different dog compared with the one she adopted and can now scratch her ears, climb the stairs and run effortlessly.

Her health has vastly improved and her loud, disruptive snoring has also disappeared.

Caroline thinks her pet now feels like a puppy again because she can be active for the first time in years.

She has started using Instagram to track Snuffy's weight loss and amassed more than 300 followers.

Snuffy takes a breather while out walking with Caroline. The porky pup lost almost 25 pounds

Snuffy in her porkier days. Her previous owners couldn't resist feeding her decadent meals

Caroline stepped in to adopt Snuffy after her previous owners, whom she had known for years, rang her to say they were moving away. 

'My apartment is on the third floor of our building and at the beginning, I really thought she was going to have a heart attack going up them,' she said.

'I knew I would have to change her lifestyle for her health. She used to love deli meat like turkey with her meal. Now she only eats twice a day.

'For the first few months I cut out treats completely, but because she's done so well I give her about five every week now.

'We go out for walks a few times a day too and longer ones at the weekend. She's done so well, she's lost 24.5lbs.

'When I first got her she used to snore like a grown man and she wasn't even able to scratch because she couldn't reach.

'Now she can lift her head and roll into her back without losing her breath.'

Snuffy before her impressive weight loss. She is on track to slim down even more for the summer

She added: 'The vet is thrilled with her progress, and hopefully, she will hit her goal weight of 30lbs by the summer.

'She's been getting a lot of support from her followers on Instagram which has been so funny.

'They say things like, "I'm amazed by this before pic" and "Great job Snuffy".

'I think she genuinely feels like a puppy again.'
