These zebras create a mind-boggling sight... so how many are there? 

Published: 17:05 EDT, 3 April 2018 | Updated: 17:05 EDT, 3 April 2018

Lined up neatly at a watering hole, these zebras make for a befuddling puzzle – just how many of them are there? 

As their striking stripes blend together, they create a mind-boggling sight, thought to be a trick to confuse predators. 

It certainly makes it hard to count them.

The herd was snapped yesterday by Trevor Barnett, 56, in Kruger National Park, South Africa. 

Look closely enough and you’ll see there are nine zebras in total – but just don’t ask us to count the stripes…
