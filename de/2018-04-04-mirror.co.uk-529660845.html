How Aubameyang and Lacazette can play together

Arsene Wenger has every faith record signings Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang and Alexandre Lacazette can play together.

France striker Lacazette was recruited last summer for a club record £46.5million, but after going through something of a goal drought and Arsenal deciding to sell Olivier Giroud, the Gunners decided to sign Aubameyang in January for £57m.

Shortly after the Gabon striker joined, Lacazette picked up an injury in his left knee and had to undergo surgery.

The 26-year-old returned to action on Sunday to score in his side’s 3-0 win over Stoke.

Arsenal won a penalty, but instead of Aubameyang completing his hat-trick the former Borussia Dortmund star let his team-mate take the spot kick.

When asked after the clash if both strikers can play in the same team, Wenger responded: “Yes, I think they can play together.

“Aubameyang has played a long part of his career on the left. And when [Robert] Lewandowski was at Dortmund he was on the left.”
