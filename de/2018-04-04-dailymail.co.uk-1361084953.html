Watch as this nail salon does an amazing pedicure transformation

An Instagram video of a rather gruesome toenail transformation has gone viral with some fans thinking the clip is 'disgusting' and others calling it a 'miracle makeup transformation but on feet'.  

Posted by a Moscow-based nail salon, Nail Sunny, the brief clip shows the rather impressive transformation of one of its client's toenails, revealing how a beautician repaired and then covered up the rigid, fungus-infected nails in order to create a fresh pedicure.

The time-lapse video, which currently has 374,897 views, shows every aspect of the pedicure process, from taking off the grown out purple nail polish to cutting down the uneven toenails.

Using various different tools, the nail technician - who is off-camera throughout the video - starts by removing the purple sparkly nail polish that is so far grown out, the bottom portion of the client's toenail is showing.   

After the polish is removed, the video cuts to the nail technician cutting the nails with a pair of nail scissors and then smoothing the cuticles with a buffer. 

The nail technician then removes the dead skin and paints the big toe with a light pink gel color before smoothing it out with a brush and overlaying a top coat of polish. 

The video then pans to shows the client's other digits while the nail technician dries them with a fan.  

And before finishing the miraculous transformation, the beautician adds the final touches, which includes painting copper glitter polish on the lunula of the toenails.

The nail technician then paints a final coat on top of the nails to give it some extra shine before showing off the final product, which is a vast improvement from the original pedicure.

But some people didn't feel the same way and couldn't get past the toe fungus. One person commented: 'Nasty with that fungus' and another person said: 'I'm disgusted. I hope there salon has a dermatologist on staff. They need it.'

But this isn't the first time Nail Sunny has dealt with clients that have toe fungus. 

Back in January, the nail salon shared a similar video that got more than one million views on Instagram. 

The video was captioned: 'Yay or nay? Nail Sunny Transformation. Be careful but if you have this problem - come to Nail Sunny.'

They also shared in the caption that the reason for the customer's destroyed nails was from wearing uncomfortable shoes and if you wear uncomfortable shoes, this can happen to you.

The founders of the amazing salon which does a ton of different transformation manicures and pedicures are young sisters Eleonora and Arina Movsisyan who lived in Los Angeles and took their experiences from the nail and beauty trends there, and brought them to Moscow.
