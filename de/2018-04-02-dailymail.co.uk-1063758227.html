As many of us begin our annual spring clean, experts have revealed exactly how often we should be cleaning our every day items.

From your make-up brushes to your mattress, there are many items around the house that need a thorough clean - probably more often than you think.

The experts at Mattress Online have created a series of handy infographics to show exactly how often you should clean and wash a variety of household items. 

Your make-up brushes, for example, should be cleaned once a week using a cleanser or shampoo.

This is because a nasty bacteria called streptococcus can live in the bristles or on a sponge, and cause illnesses such as strep throat, scarlet fever and impetigo.

Meanwhile, your mattress should be vacuumed once a fortnight, while your curtains and cushion covers should be washed once every three months.

You also shouldn't forget to replace some household items regularly - including your toothbrush every three to six months, and your toilet brush every six months.
