Diver swims underwater with a huge school of dolphins

  • The dolphins seemed completely at ease in the company of the rider 
  • A Seabob is an electric underwater scooter which is designed to explore the sea 

Published: 14:45 EDT, 29 March 2018 | Updated: 15:44 EDT, 29 March 2018

This is the incredible moment a man swam underwater with a huge school of dolphins on a Seabob who seemed completely at ease with his presence. 

The breathtaking video shows a man who is riding a seabob dive into the ocean and glide along among the stunning mammals.

A Seabob is an electric underwater scooter which is designed to explore the surface and the sea beneath it.

A Seabob rider dived into the ocean and swam alongside a school of dolphins

It allows the rider to swim with the agility of a dolphin and in this case go undetected as he swims alongside the group.

The Tico Times reported that dolphins love to approach Seabobs as they typically rush to cruise with boats.

However they are even more intrigued by the smaller and quieter Seabob.

They have so much fun with them the batteries of the Seabob usually run out before the dolphin gets bored.
