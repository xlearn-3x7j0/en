Mesut Ozil receives artistic personalised painting of his Arsenal days

  • Mesut Ozil took time out of his duties to meet up with artist Amandeep Singh
  • Ozil is gearing up for Arsenal's Premier League clash with Stoke on Sunday
  • The playmaker was rested for Germany's friendly with Brazil in midweek

Published: 08:40 EDT, 30 March 2018 | Updated: 08:52 EDT, 30 March 2018

Mesut Ozil may be the artist on the football pitch but the Arsenal man has met his match off it.

The German playmaker has been welcomed back to London Colney following the international break by receiving an impressive personalised painting of his Arsenal days.

Ozil took to Instagram on Friday afternoon to post a photo of him holding up the painting alongside popular artist Amandeep Singh.

Mesut Ozil has received this personalised painting from popular artist Amandeep Singh

And it's fair to say that the former Real Madrid star is a huge fan of his latest gift, writing on his social media page: 'Thanks for this! Absolutely love it. Keep up the great work!' 

Singh, who has a big following on Instagram, has an impressive collection of paintings. 

He has demonstrated his artistic flair through musings of music stars such as The Weeknd, Drake and DJ Khaled.

Meanwhile, Ozil is currently gearing up for Arsenal's Premier League clash with Stoke on Sunday afternoon at the Emirates.

Germany playmaker Ozil returned to Arsenal early during the international break 

The 29-year-old returned to Arsenal early during the international break after Joachim Low rested his midfielder for Germany's friendly with Brazil in Berlin.

And Arsene Wenger will be hoping that the extra break for his star man will do Ozil the world of good as Arsenal look to finish the season strongly.

The Gunners, who look set to miss out on a top four spot come the end of the season, will be desperate for Europa League glory after progressing to the quarter-finals of the competition.

The north London outfit face CSKA Moscow in the last-eight, with the first-leg being played at the Emirates on Thursday.
