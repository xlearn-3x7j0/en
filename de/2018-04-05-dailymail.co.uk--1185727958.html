Tiffany Trump poses for a pretty portrait at the White House

Tiffany Trump might be one of the few Trump family members not directly involved with working at the White House, but that doesn't mean she is flying under the radar.

The youngest daughter of the president recently appeared at the White House Easter Egg Roll on Monday, where she was not only snapped outside enjoying the festivities, but also inside the famous Blue Room by official White House photographer Shealah Craighead.

Wearing the same white blazer dress she was seen sporting on the lawn, Tiffany posed beneath the parlor's famous blue curtains for the photo.

While not attending the event with her daughter Tiffany, Donald Trump's second ex-wife Marla Maples did some of her own celebrating this week after a friend informed her that her name had made an appearance on Jeopardy. 

Marla added the news to her Instagram Story on Wednesday, saying that she learned about it through Orlando Weekly food critic Faiyaz Kara.

'I woke up to this text from my friend @faiyazkara: OMG you were on JEOPARDY tonight!!!' Marla wrote, including a screenshot from the show with the question reading: 'Mom to Tiffany Trump: Marla ____.'

Tiffany's appearance at the White House Easter event was a rare one for her, unlike her sister Ivanka, who is a regular guest at most of her father's official events. This time however, Ivanka skipped the festivities to vacation in Wyoming with her husband Jared and their children. 

The businesswoman's absence did not seem to put a dampener on the day, however; indeed Tiffany looked to be having a ball as she was seen smiling and laughing while enjoying the sights and sounds of the annual event alongside her other siblings Eric, 34, Donald Jr, 40, and Barron, 12.

Clearly keen to embrace the season, Tiffany opted for a seemingly Easter-themed outfit, wearing a white blazer dress underneath a pastel pink coat, which helped to add a fun pop of color to her look.

She finished off her outfit with a pair of nude stilettos, and wore her long hair down for the event.

Tiffany also stayed close to Donald Jr and Vanessa's children, 10-year-old Kai, Donald III, nine, Tristan, six, Spencer, five, and Chloe, three. 

Meanwhile, Ivanka, 36, and her husband Jared Kushner, 37, are currently vacationing in Wyoming after leaving on President Trump's private plane, Trump Force One, on Thursday.

The plane touched down at Cheyenne Regional Airport as the couple made their way to Saratoga, the Wyoming Tribute Eagle reported.

It will remain there until the family goes back home, according to the newspaper.

This is the second public appearance Tiffany has made in a week as part of her father's presidential duties. Unlike Ivanka, Tiffany does not hold a position in President Trump's administration, and isn't seen by his side as frequently as her older sibling. 

Last week, she and Ivanka both showed up to support their father during his trip to Cleveland, Ohio. They were seen arriving at Cleveland Hopkins International Airport, taking shelter under the same umbrella on the tarmac, as their father also touched down.

On Sunday, Tiffany was also part of the Trump family's Easter celebration at Mar-a-Lago, the president's estate in Palm Beach, Florida.

She arrived with her father and First Lady Melania Trump for Easter services at the Episcopal Church of Bethesda-by-the-Sea, and posed for photos with the couple.

Tiffany traveled to Palm Beach aboard Air Force One last Thursday, and returned on the presidential aircraft on Sunday.
