Liverpool legend makes incredible likeness to Salah and Firmino's partnership

Liverpool legend Ian Rush has compared Mohamed Salah and Roberto Firmino’s partnership to his understanding with Kenny Dalglish.

Rush says Salah and Firmino have the same telepathic understanding that he had with Kop great Dalglish back in their glory days.

Ex-Liverpool hero Rush, who has received a Legends of Sport award, told beIN Sports: “I think Salah, Firmino and Mane are all fantastic players

“I think you can see what they are doing it’s amazing, I had the same thing with Kenny Dalglish.

“I can see similar things with Salah and Firmino, they both know what to do. You just automatically run into that position. And the ball will be there and I think they have exactly the same combination.”

Rush insists though that Dalglish remains the greatest player in Liverpool’s history, largely because of his passing ability and vision.

He added: “That’s why Kenny Dalglish is the best player Liverpool have ever had. When he first told me he would put the ball there I just laughed and I didn’t make the run.

“Then he put the ball exactly where he said he could. He was a special sort of player for me, absolutely incredible.

“So the next time I said to myself I’m going to get myself in that position to do that and Kenny wouldn’t even look. He’d just put the ball in that situation and that’s what made me the player I was.

“I think time will tell (for Salah and Firmino). I think it’s the first season for them all. But I think if you ask me in another 2 or 3 seasons and they are still doing it then hopefully I’m going to say: yes!”
