Anthony Joshua admits victory against Joseph Parker could make him cry

Published: 09:08 EDT, 30 March 2018 | Updated: 09:17 EDT, 30 March 2018

Anthony Joshua tipped the scales at 17st 4lbs 2oz at the weigh-in on Friday ahead of his unification clash with Joseph Parker - a stone lighter than he was for his last fight against Carlos Takam.

There was a lot of anticipation that the WBA, IBF and IBO world title holder would come in at a lighter weight in a tactical ploy to help nullify his opponent's superior handspeed.

Parker also came in lighter, weighing in at 16st 12lbs 7oz - almost nine pounds off his weight from his title defence against Tyson Fury's cousin Hughie back in September.
