Live stream information, team news, odds and more for Juventus vs Real Madrid

Real Madrid and Juventus go head to head in a copy of last season's final.

Los Blancos triumphed at the Principality Stadium in Cardiff last May, with Cristiano Ronaldo grabbing a brace with Casemiro and Marco Asensio also getting on the score sheet, as Real became the first side to regain the Champions league.

This year has been more difficult for Zinedine Zidane's side, having struggled in La Liga before finishing runners-up to Tottenham in the group stage - with Borussia Dortmund, APOEL and Paris Saint-Germain also seen off.

Juve are in a tight title race with Napoli in Seria A but just about got past Spurs in the round of 16, with heroics at Wembley.

They had previously finished behind Barcelona in the group stage but ahead of Sporting Lisbon and Olympiacos.
