Inclement weather postpones Tigers-Royals game

DETROIT — Wednesday’s scheduled Tigers game against the Kansas City Royals at Comerica Park has been postponed because of inclement weather conditions.

The game has been rescheduled as part of a split doubleheader April 20, with the today’s makeup beginning at 1:10 p.m. and the originally scheduled game starting at 7:10 p.m. Both games of the doubleheader will be televised on FOX Sports Detroit.

All paid tickets from Wednesday’s game are valid for entry for the first game of the split doubleheader on April 20. No ticket exchange is necessary.

Please visit tigers.com for more information.
