What happened to Ronaldo before Juventus game makes bicycle kick even better

Cristiano Ronaldo scored one of the best goals of his career when he produced a stunning bicycle kick against Juventus on Tuesday evening.

The Real Madrid forward doubled his side's lead in Turin in a way that no one could ever have imagined.

And following the match, the story behind his strike in Real Madrid's 3-0 Champions league win took yet another twist.

Just 24 hours earlier, Los Blancos' number seven had been snapped practising the very same overhead technique.

But where there were pictures there is now video - and the story takes yet another turn.

Footage from the training session shows that Ronaldo actually missed his bicycle kick in training. The ball bounced off the turf before ricocheting off the post.

The five-time Ballon d'Or winner is then seen sat on the turf, visibly upset that he missed his chance.

But it makes Ronaldo's goal against Juventus that much better.

Despite knowing he had missed the last time he tried, Ronaldo still had the confidence to give it another go...in a Champions League quarter-final.

The world's best player proving that if at first you don't succeed then trying again is always the right option.
