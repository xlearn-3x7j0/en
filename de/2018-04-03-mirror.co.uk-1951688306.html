David De Gea checks in for training as speculation about his future rumbles on

David De Gea arrived for Manchester United training as speculation about his future continues to rumble on.

The Spaniard has been long linked with a move to La Liga giants Real Madrid, with the rumours set to spill over into yet another summer of uncertainty.

Madrid are looking for a replacement for Keylor Navas and see De Gea or Chelsea shotstopper Thibaut Courtois as their two main targets.

The United number one has been in fine form once again this season and the Red Devils would love nothing more than for him to stay at Old Trafford.

Reports this week suggest the club are willing to tie him down to a new deal making him the second highest paid player behind Alexis Sanchez, with a reported £350,000-a-week contract being lined up.

His current deal runs out at the end of the season, and albeit there is an option for another year, the uncertainty surrounding his future has European giants circling.

A host of other Man United stars arrived for training ahead of the Manchester Derby this weekend in which their rivals City can clinch the Premier League.

Jesse Lingard looked relaxed, whilst Luke Shaw gave the camera a wave amidst his also uncertain future.

Paul Pogba turned up in a matte black Rolls Royce with a customised red trim, and the Frenchman said this week that being benched has made him stronger.

“It's true [being benched] has never happened to me, but it can strengthen me," he told Telefoot . "You have to go through these periods to make you stronger."
