LISTEN: The Debate podcast - Craig Bellamy and Gordon Strachan

Last Updated: 04/04/18 11:21pm

Listen to the latest edition of The Debate podcast as Craig Bellamy and Gordon Strachan discussed all the big talking points.

The show started with Bellamy and Strachan paying tribute to former England captain Ray Wilkins, who died at the age of 61 on Wednesday.

There was also talk about Liverpool's 3-0 victory over Manchester City in the first leg of their Champions League quarter-final.

Bellamy and Strachan also mulled over Manchester United's approach to facing City on Saturday, live on Sky Sports, and Martin Tyler joined the show to reflect on his memories of Wilkins.
