Baby rhino tries to protect his mother while vets treat her foot

  • The baby rhino was filmed trying to push the two vets away as they treat her foot 
  • The young rhino can be seen charging at the pair, although it is pushed back 
  • Vets can be seen trying to clean the toe of the rhino and distract the baby rhino 

Published: 04:40 EDT, 4 April 2018 | Updated: 09:39 EDT, 4 April 2018

This overprotective baby rhino did not want his mother to be touch as he was filmed trying to push two vets away.

The adorable footage shows the baby rhino snuffling and running with his head down into the pair as they tried to treat it's mothers dislocated toe.

As she stood nearby, the baby can be seen watching as his mother is treated.

Then every so often, as the vets get too close for the baby's comfort, he charges at the pair in an attempt to fend them off.

The baby rhino, who does not yet have a fully grown horn, is ushered back by one of the men who tries to occupy it by putting his hand near its face while the other man cleans the toe.

While her baby is busy trying to fend off the vets, the mother rhino is laid out on the ground and appears oblivious to the fuss her offspring is causing.
