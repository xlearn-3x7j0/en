Chiellini reveals his plan to deal with Ronaldo

Giorgio Chiellini will face Real Madrid for the first time since Juventus' crushing 4-1 Champions League final defeat on Wednesday - and he insists his side have a plan to deal with Cristiano Ronaldo.

The Portugal captain scored twice on that night in Cardiff and is currently in one of the best goalscoring forms of his life.

Los Blancos' number seven has been on fire since the turn of the year, scoring 21 goals in 11 games.

A repeat fixture of last year's final will now take place in this year's last eight, with the first leg being played in Turin, Italy.

Speaking ahead of the huge tie, Chiellini said: "Lots of teams have conceded goals to Cristiano.

"He is someone who can make a difference and we’ll look to contain him, even if we know that Real Madrid have many other dangerous players."

In terms of facing Real Madrid for the first time since last year's final, Juventus' defender added: "We’re not out for revenge against Real Madrid," he insisted.

"We also have positive memories against the top Champions League teams.

"If you want to go all the way in this competition, you have to come up against opposition of this calibre."
