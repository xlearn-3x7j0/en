The perfume you should wear to match your partner's star sign

If you're looking to find love, you could give your chances a significant boost simply by wearing the right sort of perfume. 

Research by bath and shower experts Showerstoyou.co.uk has revealed which perfumes and aftershaves men and women find most alluring on each other, according to their star signs. 

Experts analysed character traits associated with each sign as well as surveying 1,440 people about their favourite scents on the opposite sex and noting their zodiac sign. 

It turns out that if you want to attract the Aries man a spicy and sweet scent such as Tom Ford's Black Orchid will reel him in, while the Gemini woman can't resist the aromatic green notes of Hugo Boss. 

Researchers have found the scents that are most attractive to the opposite sex for each star sign (stock image)

ARIES: March 21 – April 19

Men: Bold and fiery Aries is attracted to warm spicy and sweet notes

Ideal perfume: Black Orchid by Tom Ford

Women: The Aries woman enjoys citrus and fresh spicy notes on a partner

Ideal aftershave: Terre d’Hermes

Warm spicy and sweet notes in Black Orchid by Tom Ford attract Aries men (left). Citrus and fresh spicy notes in Terre d’Hermes are ideal to lure Aries women (right)

Ideal perfume: Vol de Nuit by Guerlain 

Women:  Woody aromatic notes are the ideal aphrodisiac. 

Ideal aftershave: Bleu de Chanel by Chanel
