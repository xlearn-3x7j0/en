Australia suffer final humiliation on nightmare tour of South Africa

Published: 05:29 EDT, 3 April 2018 | Updated: 05:33 EDT, 3 April 2018

Australia's nightmare tour of South Africa ended in the ignominy of a catastrophic batting collapse and a humiliating defeat by 492 runs in Johannesburg.

Vernon Philander inflicted the damage on the morning of the fifth day, taking six wickets in a devastating spell as Australia collapsed miserably from 88 for three overnight to 119 all out.

It is the fourth-heaviest runs defeat in Test cricket history - the heaviest since 1934 - and seals South Africa's 3-1 series win.

The defeat represents the final indignity on a tour that has seen the credibility of Australian cricket demolished following a ball-tampering scandal that implicated some of their leading players.
