Internet singles furious as Tinder goes OFFLINE after Facebook update

Dating app Tinder has gone down, leaving legions of internet singles in despair at being unable to swipe or access their messages.

Changes to Facebook's Application Programming Interface (API) locked Tinder users out of their accounts around the globe before the glitch was fixed.

Furious Tinder devotees took to Twitter to vent their anger, reserving their harshest criticism for Facebook CEO Mark Zuckerberg.
