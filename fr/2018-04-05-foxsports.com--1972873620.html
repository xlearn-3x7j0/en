PROGRAMMING ALERT: Channel listings for Tampa Bay Lightning vs. Buffalo Sabres on April 6

TAMPA, Fla. (April 5, 2018) — FOX Sports Sun, the regional television home of the Tampa Bay Lightning, announced the Lightning vs. Buffalo Sabres game on Friday, April 6 will be made available on the FOX Sports Sun alternate channel for select television providers. The broadcast will feature Inside the Lightning Live at 6:30 PM and the Lightning Live pregame show a half hour before puck drop, in addition to streaming live on FOX Sports GO.

Below please find alternate channel numbers on which each game can be found in the Tampa Bay television market:

DISH CH. # 445 DISH HD CH. # 9515 DISH HOPPER CH. # 412-33 DTV CH. # 653-1 DTV HD CH. # 653-1 AT&T CH. # 723 AT&T HD CH. # 1723

The game will appear on the regular FOX Sports Sun channel for all providers not listed above. For more information on the FOX Sports Florida and FOX Sports Sun alternate channels, click here.

ABOUT FOX SPORTS FLORIDA / FOX SPORTS SUN
