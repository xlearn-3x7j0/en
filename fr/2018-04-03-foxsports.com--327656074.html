Ducks' Cam Fowler out 2-6 weeks with shoulder injury

Anaheim Ducks defenseman Cam Fowler will miss 2-6 weeks with a shoulder injury, the team announced Tuesday.

Fowler suffered the injury during Sunday’s 4-3 overtime win over the Colorado Avalanche– a game he registered a team-high 25:41 on the ice despite not playing the extra time.

The 26-year-old netted eight goals and accounted for 24 assists in 67 games so far this season after missing 12 games with a knee injury earlier in the year.

The Ducks currently sit two points ahead of the Avalanche for the first wild card spot with three games remaining in the regular season.
