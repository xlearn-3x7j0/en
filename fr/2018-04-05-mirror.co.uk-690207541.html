Tiger Woods hole-by-hole analysis from Masters 2018 first round

Tiger Woods is back at the Masters and huge crowds greeted his arrival at the first tee and around the Augusta National.

Dressed in all black under a clear blue sky, the four-time champion got off to a solid if unspectacular start playing with England's Tommy Fleetwood and Marc Leishman of Australian.

The 14-time Major winner, playing the Masters for the 21st time, was one-over par after nine holes in tied 23rd as he trails his playing partner and early leader Leishman by four shots.

Since Woods played his first Masters as a professional in 1997, none of his playing partners has ever held a lead at the end of a Masters round in his company.

Fleetwood nearly holed out for an eagle at No.8 but birdied the hole to move back to level par.

Woods famously posted four-over par 40 in his first nine holes in 1997 before romping to a 12-shot win.

No.1: Tea Olive, Par 4, 445 yards

Despite using a 3-wood off the tee, Woods pulls his opening tee shot into trees on the left. Threads his second through a gap to the front fringe of the green and lags up a 75-foot putt for a tap-in par.

No. 2: Pink Dogwood, Par 5, 575 yards

Woods walks his big dog on the second with a thunderous drive to leave him 219 yards from the pin. But he pushes a long iron into a greenside bunker and cannot make the curling 12-foot birdie putt. He has never eagled this hole.

No.3: Flowering Peach, Par 4, 350 yards

Another huge drive left the four-time champion only 20 yards short of the green. He chipped up to eight feet and rolled in the birdie putt to get into the red numbers.

No.4: Flowering Crab Apple, Par 3, 240 yards

The American does not remain under par for long. He finds the bunker off the tee and cannot make the 15-foot par saver. This hole is his worst in relation to par at Augusta. He has played it in 3.22 or +17.

No.5: Magnolia, Par 4, 455 yards

Woods uses 3-wood off the tee again but finds a greenside bunker. Find the green but 66 feet from the hole. Rolls first putt 8 feet past and misses on the low side to go one-over par.

No.6: Juniper, Par 3, 180 yards

He had an eight-foot birdie putt after super tee shot but repeats his error from the previous green and his putt just ran out of legs and fell off line just inches before the hole.

No.7: Pampas, Par 4, 450 yards

Smokes another 3-wood 289 yards off the tee but with the adrenaline flowing, he hits his approach shot 36 feet beyond the flag and the birdie putts slips by.

No.8: Yellow Jasmine, Par 5, 570 yards

This time he pings his tee shot 303 yards but his second is short and left and his chip goes 30 feet past the pin as he fails to pick up a shot at the second par five on the front nine.

No. 9: Carolina Cherry, Par 4, 460 yards

Trying to draw his driver off the tee, Woods shouts: 'No, no' at his ball as he finds the second cut – the first time he has missed the fairway with his driver. He was still able to find the green but could not drain a 36-foot birdie putt.
