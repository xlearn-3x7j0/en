Masters: Serious injuries as bus crashes on way to Augusta

By Keith Jackson at Augusta National

Last Updated: 05/04/18 7:20pm

Several patrons heading to the Masters have been seriously injured after their bus overturned on the main interstate heading to Augusta National.

The bus was carrying 18 passengers with tickets to watch the first round when the accident happened at around 8:45am local time on Interstate 20.

Eyewitnesses reported that the bus veered off the road before the driver attempted a sharp correction, which led to the vehicle flipping over and sliding to a halt.

The 61-year-old driver, named by Georgia State Patrol officers as Steven Hoppenbrouwer, has been arrested and charged with a DUI offence.

Augusta University Medical Center is believed to be treating five patients for serious injuries, with another two reported to be in fair condition.

But further reports suggest other passengers are in critical condition at a different hospital.
