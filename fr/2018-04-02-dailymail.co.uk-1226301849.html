Well-dressed man waits at carousel for suitcase and a RUBBISH BIN

  • A fellow traveler filmed the man waiting casually for his unorthodox luggage 
  • He wrapped a wheelie bin in a garbage bag and added straps to the outside
  • Surprisingly the 'luggage' bin was cleared and made it to its destination 
  • Facebook users seemed to enjoy the odd sight with some ready to try it too  

Published: 10:34 EDT, 2 April 2018 | Updated: 10:44 EDT, 2 April 2018

A smartly dressed man has taken recycling and re-purposing to new levels after he was snapped waiting for his unorthodox luggage at an airport pick up. 

In a video posted on social media, a man is seen waiting patiently at baggage claim when his luggage starts to come around. 

It takes a second to notice but then it becomes plainly obvious.

A smartly dressed man has taken recycling and re-purposing to new levels after he was snapped waiting for his unorthodox luggage at an airport pick up

The man has wrapped a garbage bag around the top of a green wheelie bin, attached a cord as a makeshift strap and turned it into luggage. 

Possibly most surprising, is the luggage was accepted and the man was able to receive it upon his arrival. 

The post garnered 19,000 views in less than an hour.

Facebook users saw the amusing side of the video. 

'Put a ribbon on it so you don't get confused with other peoples bins,' one user wrote. 

'Both practicable and durable... I think he onto something here,' another wrote. 

Others decided the idea was so smart they might just have to give it a go themselves. 

'Why did we spend all that money on cases last week when we have 2 perfectly good bins?' one user wrote.
