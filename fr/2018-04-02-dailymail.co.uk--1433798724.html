Parents troll naughty kids by making them walk in their car seats

  • A video shows two kids trying to walk while still strapped to their car seats
  • It's unclear if they were being punished or their parents were just moving the car
  • The video was filmed by a bystander at a Missouri car wash 

Published: 13:08 EDT, 2 April 2018 | Updated: 13:08 EDT, 2 April 2018

This Missouri parents seem to have a very peculiar way of teaching their kids a lesson.

A video filmed by a bystander shows the moment three children are left behind as their parents slowly drive away at a car wash.

Two of them are seen wobbling as they attempt to follow their big sister and catch up to their parents' car while still strapped to their car seats.

Better hurry! A video caught the moment two kids strapped to their car seat try to walk

As the SUV picks up speed, so does the little girl, picking up the pace and looking back to see if her siblings are keeping up.

Thankfully, the car comes to a stop after a few seconds and the little girl is then seen throwing snow in the air. 

'I had to video this,' said the person behind the camera.

Whether the parents meant to play a prank on their kids or were simply moving from one service station to the other remains unknown.
