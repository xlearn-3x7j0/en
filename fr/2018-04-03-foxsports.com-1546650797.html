Cardinals place Gyorko on DL, recall Bader from Memphis

Jedd Gyorko is out with a right hamstring strain.

Benny Sieu-USA TODAY Sports

Cardinals fans wanting to see outfielder Harrison Bader in a St. Louis uniform will get their wish after his recall from Triple-A Memphis — but at the expense of infielder Jedd Gyorko.

The Cardinals placed Gyorko on the 10-day disabled list with a right hamstring strain he suffered legging out an infield single Monday at Milwaukee.

Bader, 23, was the Cardinals’ Minor League Player of the Year in 2017. He hit .283 with 20 home runs in 117 games at Memphis.

Bader made his major league debut last season, hitting three home runs in his first 11 games and playing in 32 games overall.
