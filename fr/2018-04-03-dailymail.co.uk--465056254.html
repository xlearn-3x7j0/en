Woman documents her weight loss journey after becoming vegan

A 20-year-old who weighed over 200 pounds has revealed how she managed to shed 35 pounds in a matter of months after giving up fast food in favor of a strict vegan diet.  

Rekik Neway, from Los Angeles, spent years indulging in daily fast food fixes, particularly from her favorite, Popeye's, before embarking on her weight loss journey in April 2017.

And it wasn't just her weight that was affected by her diet; Rekik was also suffering from severe stomach pains as a result of her unhealthy food intake, and slowly began cutting out different foods to combat this, eventually reducing her weight to just 167.8 pounds by August that same year.

Rekik managed to completely transform her body in just four months when she admits she had an epiphany in April and lost 35 pounds going from 202.4 pounds to 167.8 pounds. 

Describing how she feels about losing weight Rekik shared, 'I have never worked this hard for something.' 

She was just getting out of a relationship and was at her heaviest weight of 202.2 pounds at 19-years-old.

'That was the time that I actually had the courage to weigh myself because I knew I let myself go,' she confessed. 

Realizing she was letting herself go, Rekik admits that she finally had a wake-up call and said, 'I wanted to challenge myself and all the buzzers went off in my head.'

Rekik shared that she wanted to challenge herself to prove herself wrong, 'I wanted to stop giving myself false hope because I kept falling off time and time again.' 

'I had been blinded in certain aspects of my life. I had slacked and I was eating out almost every single day and being careless with my health, body and well-being,' she said.

Once she realized it was time to kick it into gear she started going to the gym six days a week and was there every single morning at five a.m. admitting, 'I really wanted to teach myself discipline.'

When she went to the gym everyday she would spend an hour 'going hard' by running over three miles on the treadmill followed by the stair master. 

After going to the gym Rekik began seeing some results but more importantly she felt good and even said, 'Things started looking up in my life and I realized it was because I was putting in work.' 

As soon as she got into a routine of waking up early and hitting the gym she said to herself, 'I have no reason to stop now.'

While everything seemed to be going well for her and she was finally motivated to lose weight, she endured severe stomach pains and wasn't seeing any results.

She described the pains saying, 'I would be in pure discomfort and every time I ate my stomach would react.'

Not only was her stomach pain getting worse she would constantly look and feel bloated which she thinks could've been from her poor diet.

Rekik explained that her eating habits were terrible and even joked, 'Tuesdays at Popeye's I was there. The rest of the week, I was there too.'

When the discomfort became too much Rekik wanted to find the root of the issue of what was making her stomach feel so uncomfortable after she ate.

So in August she completely cut out dairy products which she admitted was quite hard for her because she has a sweet tooth and loves ice cream.

Once she cut out dairy she would only get dairy-free products but admitted she couldn't resist some sweets despite becoming very sensitive to dairy products.

'If I see a cookie I eat that. Creamy chocolate cake I devour that,' she joked. 

After cutting dairy out of her diet she slowly started taking things out of her diet and had stopped eating beef around April.

Ever since she cut out beef she just stuck to chicken and turkey before eventually cutting away from chicken.

She admits that she was all over the place and her diet was wacky until she read the book 22-Day Revolution by Marco Borges.

The book gives you a 22-day vegan meal plan that encourages vegans to also become gluten free which she did and gushed about the diet saying it's, 'life changing.'

Another major impact on her weight loss was drinking at least one bottle of water before eating any meal which she does to control her portions and make herself full so that she doesn't overeat.

Rekik, who loved Popeye's and was a meat-eater with absolutely no restrictions on her diet managed to change her lifestyle and embrace it. 

Even she admitted it was crazy, 'Never in my life did I think I would be able to go vegan. Shook, I am.'

Cutting out meat and dairy were the two main factors of her weight loss but she confessed it wasn't easy.

'If you really commit to something like working out and being conscious of your food intake, change will come.

'Becoming healthier wasn't about wanting to be skinny this time around, it was truly to just feel good and happy with myself. 

'I can't explain how miserable and defeated I was in the before picture but, my face says it all.  

'It's been a crazy learning experience watching my body change these past four months,' she said.

As for what's next in her future, Rekik couldn't be more excited as she gushed, 'There's so many things I've wanted to wear and do but I've never felt comfortable enough to do it.'
