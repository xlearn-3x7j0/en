All you need to know about Logan Paul vs KSI boxing fight

It's boxing - but not as we know it.

YouTube sensation KSI has signed terms on a two-fight deal with fellow social media star Logan Paul.

KSI called out his controversial fellow social media star after his win over Joe Weller in London and Paul released a video on YouTube accepting the challenge.

Paul, who has more than 16million subscribers on YouTube, hit the headlines earlier this year when he filmed a suicide victim during a trip to Japan's 'suicide forest'.

He has since apologised and is expected to trade leather with KSI who enjoys a YouTube fanbase of over 18m.

No date has been set for the fight yet but it is expected to take place in August in 2018.

The pair are set to fight first in Manchester before a showdown in the States.
