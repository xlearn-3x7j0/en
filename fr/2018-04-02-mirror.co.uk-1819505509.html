Full list of Easter Monday postponed games as weather plays havoc with fixtures

Adverse weather conditions have played havoc with the bank holiday fixture list with a clutch of games on Easter Monday falling by the wayside.

Some parts of the UK have been battered by heavy rain and snow which has seen a number of fixtures fall foul of the weather.

A full card of matches were scheduled in the Championship, League One and League Two, but several games have been postponed both in the Football League and non-league.

The highest profile casualty thus far is the Championship clash between Nottingham Forest and Barnsley, which has fallen foul of a waterlogged pitch.

Here are all the postponements...

Nottingham Forest vs Barnsley
