Football and racing fixtures postponed due to unseasonal weather

Last Updated: 02/04/18 12:50pm

Unseasonal weather has played havoc with Easter Monday's fixture list with several football matches and race meetings washed out.

A number of Sky Bet Football League games have been postponed including the Championship clash between Nottingham Forest and Barnsley.

Monday afternoon's race meetings at Chepstow, Fakenham, Market Rasen, Huntingdon, Redcar and Plumpton have been abandoned due to waterlogging, while Tuesday's programme has also been badly hit.

Postponed matches

Sky Bet Championship

Nottingham Forest v Barnsley

Sky Bet League One

Bradford City v Walsall
