England beat Scotland in Commonwealth Games netball opener

Last Updated: 05/04/18 10:25am

England thrashed Scotland 74-28 in their opening group netball match at the 2018 Commonwealth Games on Thursday morning.

But Serena Guthrie was forced off injured in the opening quarter, giving England a selection headache for their next Pool B fixtures.

England play Malawi on Friday, Uganda on Sunday, Wales on Monday and New Zealand next Wednesday.

England had established an eight-goal lead over Scotland inside the first 15 minutes, extended to 34-14 by half-time.

England took their foot off the gas in the second half and cruised to an opening victory.
