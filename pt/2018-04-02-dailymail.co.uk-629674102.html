South African anti-apartheid activist Winnie Mandela dies aged 81

Published: 10:06 EDT, 2 April 2018 | Updated: 10:20 EDT, 2 April 2018

Winnie Madikizela-Mandela has died aged 81, her personal assistant said today.

The South African anti-apartheid activist and politician was married to Nelson Mandela for 38 years.

Her personal assistant Zodwa Zwane gave no further details but said a statement would be released later.
