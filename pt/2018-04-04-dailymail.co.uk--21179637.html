Little girl, 4, takes the internet by storm with celebrity impressions

A little girl has taken the Internet by storm after posing in a series of celebrity-inspired outfits, all handmade by her mother.

Stefanie Chaglar, four, from Antalya, Turkey, impersonates famous red carpet ensembles on Instagram - but with a home-made budget twist.

Having mimicked the likes of Lady Gaga, Rita Ora and Katy Perry, since she was just two-years-old Stephanie now has amassed a following of 55,000.

And it's thanks to the help of her creative mum, Alevtina, 32, who crafts the frocks from cheap household items, that she is becoming a tiny fashionista herself.

Speaking about her daughter's online success, Alevtina shared the secret behind her homemade designs, revealing she often uses simple kitchen products.

'I've made her outfits from paper, folio, bubble wrap and even bin bags, and they only take ten minutes to make,' she said.

'I was initially inspired by Rihanna's post on Instagram where she was wearing the ruffled ball gown and seeing Stefanie modelling makes me feel happy.

'But she loves it too - she loves modelling and the camera loves her!' she added.

Alevtina revealed Stefanie started dressing up at just two years old, and was immediately a natural poser.

'She was just two when we started to make these photographs and she has always enjoyed the entire process', she said.

'She loves dressing up and she always helps me to make new outfits for her - and every time when we finish shooting she is very excited to check if her 'dress' looks the same as the celebrity's one.

'But regardless of this, we do all of our photoshoots for fun and we don't want to be taken seriously!

'In the future, Stefanie hopes to dress as Jennifer Lopez, Nicole Kidman, Lady Gaga and Beyonce too.'
