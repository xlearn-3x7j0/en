Man shot in the head on Facebook Live stream as friend plays with gun

A Texas woman was arrested after she was captured on Facebook Live shooting a man in the head while playing with a handgun.

The horrific video, which has now been seen thousands of times on social media, shows the exact moment Devyn Holmes was shot on Sunday. 

Holmes, a 26-year-old father-of-two, is now on life support after he was shot around 2.30am in the parking lot of a Valero gas station in Houston.
