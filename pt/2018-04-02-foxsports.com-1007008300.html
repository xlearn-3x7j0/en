Report: Furman, Wofford secure future Clemson games

(STATS) – Southern Conference members Furman and Wofford have scheduled games at in-state power Clemson.

FBSchedules.com reported both games will be played at Clemson’s Memorial Stadium, with Furman visiting on Nov. 22, 2025, and Wofford on Nov. 20, 2027. Each SoCon team will receive a $500,000 guarantee, the report said.

Wofford already had games scheduled at Clemson on Nov. 2, 2019, and Sept. 2, 2023.
