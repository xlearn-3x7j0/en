Saul Canelo Alvarez WITHDRAWS from rematch against Gennady Golovkin

Published: 15:21 EDT, 3 April 2018 | Updated: 15:22 EDT, 3 April 2018

Saul 'Canelo' Alvarez has reportedly pulled out of his long-awaited rematch with middleweight world champion Gennady Golovkin.

The pair were due to meet at the T-Mobile Arena on May 5, eight months after their first clash ended in a controversial draw but now it appears that the fight will not go ahead.

The Mexican was last week placed under provisional suspension by the Nevada State Athletic Commission, who were conducting an investigation into his failed drugs test.
