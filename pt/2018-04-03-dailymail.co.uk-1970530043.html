Barcelona 'lining up' Clement Lenglet if Samuel Umtiti leaves

  • Barcelona's Samuel Umtiti has attracted heavy interest from Manchester United
  • The Nou Camp outfit have devised a back-up plan if Umtiti departs in summer
  • Clement Lenglet has been one of the revelations in La Liga this season 

Published: 09:13 EDT, 3 April 2018 | Updated: 12:21 EDT, 3 April 2018

Barcelona are eyeing a move for Sevilla defender Clement Lenglet if Samuel Umtiti leaves this summer, according to reports in Spain.

With Umtiti attracting heavy interest from Manchester United as Jose Mourinho looks to bolster his defence, the Catalan giants have devised a back-up plan to replace him.

And according to Spanish publication Sport, Barcelona are interested in bringing in 22-year-old French centre back Lenglet.

Barcelona have lined up Clement Lenglet as a potential replacement for Samuel Umtiti

Lenglet has impressed hugely in La Liga and Champions League so far this season, earning plenty of plaudits for his performances against Romelu Lukaku as Sevilla knocked United out in the last-16 stage.

Barcelona will face competition from a host of clubs to sign Sevilla star Lenglet, who has a release clause of just £26million.

However, Lenglet has recently teased of a move to the Nou Camp, describing Barcelona as a 'great team'.

Lenglet, here in action against Umtiti, recently described Barcelona as a 'great team'

Speaking to a French agency recently, Lenglet said: 'Barcelona are a great team, with international players at a very high level.

'But it's the same here (at Sevilla), too, and I am happy here. We will see what happens in the future, but for now, I am focused on La Liga and the Champions League.'

Lenglet and his Sevilla team-mates take on Bayern Munich at home on Tuesday night in the first-leg of their Champions League quarter-final.
