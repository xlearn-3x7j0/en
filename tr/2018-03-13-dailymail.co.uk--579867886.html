A self-proclaimed 'human doll' has splashed thousands on cosmetic surgery to make herself look like Barbie.

Ophelia Vanity, 30, from Los Angeles, is half-Chinese by heritage, but has spent $4,000 (£2,900) on surgery to her eyelids in an effort to make her features more 'western', like those of her idol.

As well as eyelid surgery, the Instagram star, who boasts more than 70,000 followers thanks to her photos of her extreme look, has monthly injections of Botox and fillers to maintain her doll-like appearance. She also forks out to maintain her bleached locks. 

Despite the drastic measures she has already taken, Ophelia says she still intends to get breast and bum implants, bleach her skin, and is even considering having four to six ribs removed in order to achieve a tiny waist.
