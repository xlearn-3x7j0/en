Liverpool winger Mohamed Salah tops Sky Sports Power Rankings

Last Updated: 03/04/18 8:20am

Liverpool's Mohamed Salah has topped the Sky Sports Power Rankings this week.

The Egypt international scored his 29th league goal of the season to seal a 2-1 win at Crystal Palace on Saturday, after team-mate Sadio Mane had cancelled out an early Luka Milivojevic penalty.

Tottenham midfielder Dele Alli scored twice during a 3-1 win at Chelsea to soar into the Power Rankings' top 10, with team-mate Christian Eriksen hitting a long-range stunner in first-half stoppage time.

David Silva assisted two goals during Manchester City's 3-1 victory at Everton, teeing up Leroy Sane's opener and another for Raheem Sterling to help City move within one victory of claiming the Premier League title.

Burnley striker Chris Wood scored his fourth goal in three league games with the match-winner at rock-bottom West Brom - a result that prompted Baggies boss Alan Pardew to leave the club by mutual consent on Monday.

Alexis Sanchez broke into the top 10 for the first time since his transfer to Manchester United in January after assisting Romelu Lukaku's 100th Premier League goal and scoring 15 minutes later to seal a 2-0 win against Swansea.

The Power Rankings methodology reflects a player's performance over the last five matchdays by awarding points to players for 32 different stats.
