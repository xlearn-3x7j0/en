The Masters greatest putt: Select your all-time Augusta favourites

By Sky Sports Golf

Last Updated: 04/04/18 3:32pm

Augusta National has witnessed some memorable moments through the years, but what has been the greatest putt made in Masters history?

Ahead of the opening men's major of the year, live on Sky Sports, we delved into the archives to select 16 of the best efforts for a special Twitter vote during tournament week in Georgia.

Four-time Masters winner Tiger Woods and 18-time major champion Jack Nicklaus are among the players to feature in the contest, with the putts randomly drawn into eight head-to-head match ups for viewers to vote for their favourites.

The eight winning putts will be announced on Friday lunchtime and progress to the quarter-finals, when a separate vote will determine which shots then progress to the last four.

The semi-finalists will be revealed live on the Masters Breakfast show on Saturday and another poll will be hosted during the third round to decide which two putts reach Sunday's main final.

Sunday's Masters Breakfast will confirm the two putts left in contention and launch the final poll, with the winning putt revealed during Sky Sports' coverage of the final round at Augusta.
