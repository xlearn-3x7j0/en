Bolton 0-1 Birmingham: Rare Jutkiewicz strike wins it for 10-man Blues

  • Birmingham beat Bolton with Lukas Jutkiewicz netting the winner on Tuesday
  • He turned home a free kick to net his first Championship goal since September
  • Birmingham held on for a third straight win despite seeing Che Adams sent off
  • Twenty-one-year-old Adams got a direct red for a horror tackle on Craig Noone

Published: 16:57 EDT, 3 April 2018 | Updated: 17:01 EDT, 3 April 2018

Ten-man Birmingham City beat relegation rivals Bolton Wanderers thanks to Lukas Jutkiewicz's first Championship goal since September. 

Jutkiewicz turned home a Jacques Maghoma free kick to fire the Blues in front just before half time.

And Birmingham were able to hold on for their third straight victory despite seeing Che Adams sent off for a horror take on Craig Noone midway through the second half.
