Tourist stopped for bikini in Philippines is American Instagram model

A tourist stopped by police in the Philippines for wearing skimpy bikini bottoms while on a moped has been revealed as an American Instagram model. 

Kinsey Golden, from Los Angeles, has more than 40,000 followers on the social media site and is regularly pictured in revealing outfits.

Golden was stopped by police alongside partner Trav Springer on an island in Bohol last month and ordered to cover up with a sarong.

Trav, who was shirtless at the time, told The Sun that he was not aware the pair were breaking any laws before officers pulled them over.
