Stylist files official complaint against Ed Westwick with LAPD

The fashion stylist who has accused actor Ed Westwick of being a 'violent sexual predator' has filed a report with the police in Los Angeles, DailyMail.com can reveal.

Haley Camille Freedman, 23, is the fourth woman to claim that the Gossip Girl star raped or sexually assaulted her.

She claims Westwick repeatedly raped her and held her captive for two days at his Hollywood mansion, according to court papers.

The former art director of the Instagram-based fashion magazine Kat & Bare, Freedman named Westwick in documents filed in Federal court in Los Angeles earlier this month citing the alleged rape.

And on Wednesday the blonde met with detectives at the LAPD Hollywood station to make an official complaint about the alleged attack in 2014.

In exclusive photos obtained by DailyMail.com, Freedman was seen leaving the police station where she had spent a few hours.

Dressed in blue jeans, black top and taupe jacket and wearing a black hat, Freedman looked somber as she left the police station late afternoon.

It's understood detectives already probing previous allegations against Westwick interviewed Freedman and will now investigate her claims against the star.

Sources close to the case have told DailyMail.com that officers have scheduled a meeting for Freedman to sit down with the LA County District Attorney's office next week.

One source said: 'The police are taking these allegations very seriously, detectives sat down with Haley for hours and they want her to meet with the DA next week.'

In a court filing earlier this month, Freedman said she's suing her ex-partners at fashion firm Kat & Bare for, among several complaints, breach of contract and fraud, accusing them of 'shunning' her and cutting her out of the business she helped build after she decided to go public with her shocking account of her alleged 'nightmare' sexual encounter with Westwick.

In the 129-page lawsuit, the 30-year-old screen heartthrob. - best known for playing womanizing bad boy Chuck Bass on Gossip Girl - is described by her lawyers as 'monstrous', a 'serial rapist', and 'abusive, crazed alcoholic'.

Westwick has yet to comment on Freedman's allegations, but last year, he issued a statement protesting his innocence after actresses Aurelie Wynn and Rachel Eck shared their stories online.

But earlier this week the star deleted statements he posted on social media denying sexual assault allegations made against him as the LA District Attorney's office investigates the claims.

'I have never forced myself in any manner, on any woman,' he said at the time. 'I certainly have never committed rape… I am co-operating with the authorities so that they can clear my name as soon as possible.' 

He has since taken that statement down.
