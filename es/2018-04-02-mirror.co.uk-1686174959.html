Barcelona star aware of Manchester United interest

Barcelona defender Samuel Umtiti has admitted he is aware that he is admired by Manchester United.

Jose Mourinho is known to be a big fan of the 24-year-old Cameroon centre-back whose release clause is £52.6million.

The Reds boss is planning an overhaul this summer but Umtiti has warned they could face a fight to land him.

He said: “It’s not only Man Utd, other clubs want me as well, but my sole focus is on Barcelona.

“Negotiations for a contract renewal have not started yet. My release clause is low, but that’s not what I worry about, I’m focused on the pitch.”
