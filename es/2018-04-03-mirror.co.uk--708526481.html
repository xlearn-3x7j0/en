WWE UK Tour May 2018: Show and venue details plus how to buy tickets

WWE returns to the UK this May with a post- WrestleMania tour featuring the superstars of Raw and SmackDown LIVE.

The American sports entertainment giant will visit seven locations across the British Isles on a 10-day tour from May 9 to May 18.

And it will record its flagship shows Monday Night Raw and SmackDown LIVE at London’s O2 Arena on May 14 and 15, respectively.

On the company's last UK tour in November, AJ Styles won the WWE Championship from Jinder Mahal at the Manchester Arena.

It was the first time the title had changed hands outside of North America and the first time the belt had been traded on SmackDown since 2003.

The previous evening on Raw, Seth Rollins and Dean Ambrose dropped the Raw Tag Team Championship to Sheamus and Cesaro.

The title switches showed that these dates are not to be missed as anything can happen in WWE - even on this side of the Atlantic.

Raw's finest including Roman Reigns, Braun Strowman, Finn Bálor, Seth Rollins, Alexa Bliss and Sasha Banks are expected to feature on the tour.

Meanwhile, SmackDown talent such as AJ Styles, Shinsuke Nakamura, Charlotte Flair, The New Day, Becky Lynch, Kevin Owens and Sami Zayn should compete.

Fans will hope that the recently cleared to compete Daniel Bryan and debuting WWE superstar Ronda Rousey may also be included on the jaunt.

Tour dates:

Wednesday, May 9: Bournemouth International Centre, Bournemouth, England

Thursday, May 10: SSE Arena, Belfast, Northern Ireland

Friday, May 11: 3Arena, Dublin, Ireland

Monday, May 14: Raw at O2 Arena, London, England

Tuesday, May 15: SmackDown LIVE at O2 Arena, London, England

Tuesday, May 15: Genting Arena, Birmingham, England

Wednesday, May 16: Echo Arena, Liverpool, England

Thursday, May 17: Metro Radio Arena, Newcastle, England

Friday, May 18: FlyDSA Arena, Sheffield, England

Tickets for all shows are available from www.bookingsdirect.com . For tickets for the Dublin, Ireland show, visit www.ticketmaster.ie .

WWE will also return to London’s O2 for a one-off show on August 28. Tickets are available from AXS .
