The five best mascaras to buy - and you don't have to spend a fortune!

For many women mascara is a core part of their make up routine, but some formulas are too clumpy or instantly smudge to your upper lid or lower lash line.

Now Australian celebrity make up artist, Bonnie Gillies, has shared the best mascaras to lift your lashes. 

'There are very few things more disappointing than having perfectly lifted, curved and voluminous lashes as you're leaving the house and flat, spindly lashes when you get to the office or your event,' she wrote in a blog post.

Australian celebrity make up artist, Bonnie Gillies, has revealed her top five mascaras that give the perfect elongated lash-look every time

Bonnie explained she needed her mascara to do different things at different times, depending on the make up look she was after. 

'Sometimes I might need extra length with superior definition, other times I want thick, full and fluffy lashes,' she said.

'It's easier to navigate than you’d think, as long as the mascara I'm using performs the way it promises.'

She shared the 'tricks of the trade' that help improve the performance of any formula you buy.

HOW TO IMPROVE YOUR MASCARA 

- Invest in a good eyelash primer

- Curl your lashes to make your eyes look bigger

- Always use an oil based remover to dissolve the formula

- Never scrub away at your eyes 

'Firstly, investing in a good primer can help boost your volume and improve the wear time,' Bonnie revealed.

'Secondly, always curl your lashes to lift them and give your eyes the appearance of being bigger and wider (and more awake!).

'Thirdly, removing mascara is just as important as putting it on in order to protect the health of your lashes. Always use an oil based remover that's designed to dissolve the formula to allow you to slip it off with a cotton pad.' 

L'OREAL PARADISE MASCARA – $24.95

'L'Oreal's latest addition to their family of mascaras is everything you’d expect from the drugstore brand and more,' she wrote.

'It's affordable, the brush nicely separates lashes while giving a fluffy voluminous look and it doesn't flake off during the day.'

As an added bonus, the formula is enriched with castor oil which Bonnie said was shown to improve the growth rate of your lashes.

Since the formula is moister than your regular tube, those with longer lashes may find it transfers to their skin.

SHISEIDO FULL LASH MULTI-DIMENSION WATERPROOF MASCARA – $27

This mascara is perfect for trying to get those pesky little lashes in the corner of their eyes

'Probably the most exciting thing about this mascara is the innovative wand. It bends to a 30-degree angle when gently leveraged against the top of the tube to allow you to fan and lift your lashes with ease,' Bonnie explained.

For those who are sick of trying to get those pesky little lashes in the corner of their eyes without getting the black goop everywhere, this is perfect product to help with that. 

'The formula leaves your lashes incredibly black but surprisingly, it doesn’t budge,' she continued.

'As for removal, other brands waterproof options can be notoriously hard to remove but Shiseido’s mascara came off easily with a bit of micellar water without damaging my lashes.'

LA ROCHE-POSAY RESPECTISSIME SENSITIVE VOLUME MASCARA – $29.99

Bonnie explained that for women with irritable eyes or for those with contacts, French brand La Roche-Posay, is for you.

This is because they specifically formulates all of their products for sensitive skin. 

'Their products are high quality, dermatologist approved and insanely affordable,' she wrote.

'The Respectissime Volume Mascara certainly lives up to the brands cult pharmacy brand status.

'The fluffy brush separates and bulks up lashes without making them look spindly and, the formulas not too thick so you build volume really easily without having to worry about clumping.'

BENEFIT BAD GAL BANG – $42

This mascara has been hyped up online since its release

The Bad Gal Bang mascara from Benefit has been hyped up online ever since its release.

This meant that Bonnie had high expectations of its performance. 

'It promises out of this world, gravity defying, full volume lashes and a formula that lasts 36 hours,' she said.

'While it didn’t give me the thick, fluffy volume I normally go for, my lashes were perfectly separated, insanely lengthened, bold, black and yes, they looked the same when I got home in the evening as they did when I left home.'

ELIZABETH ARDEN GRAND ENTRANCE MASCARA – $42

Although this product from Elizabeth Arden has a heftier price tag, Bonnie said that it was well worth it if you could afford it.

'It's not often I rely on a mascara to give my lashes curl because that’s what a curler is for right?' she explained.

'Well, this mascara has become one of my favourites for a lifted and curled lash that doesn’t budge all day long, without needing any help!

'The formula is quite wet giving you a lot of time to control your lashes before it dries down to a slick, bold black finish.'   

Bonnie said this mascara is worth its price tag because it lifts and curls lashes but doesn't budge all day long
