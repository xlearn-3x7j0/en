James Packer has ex-wife Jodhi Meares 'on speed-dial'

They may have ended their marriage in 2002 it seems James Packer and Jodhi Meares will  always hold a soft spot for each other.

Woman's Day reports the casino mogul has his first wife on 'speed dial' and 'calls her all the time' when he needs support.

The magazine went on to say that James, who is currently receiving treatment for mental health issues, considers Jodhi one of his most loyal supporters alongside his mother, Ros, and second wife Erica Packer.
