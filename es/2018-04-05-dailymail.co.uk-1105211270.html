Scott Disick buys mansion for $3.25 million in Southern California

It's a home fit for a king - make that, 'The Lord.'

Scott Disick, 34, recently purchased a five-bedroom, six-bathroom home located in the lush gated community of Hidden Hills, California, not too far from the Kardashian family home base of Calabasas, TMZ reported.

The debonair dad-of-three's new house, which was built 45 years ago, spans 4,934 sq. feet over an acre, and features eye-catching amenities both on the inside and outside, according to the real estate site Redfin.

The inside of the home is accented by a gorgeous open floor plan that makes use of artisan wrought iron and crown moldings.
