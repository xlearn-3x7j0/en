Masters first round leaderboard latest and Tiger Woods updates

The golf season starts here - and this glorious event is one you definitely do not want to miss.

The year's first Major gets underway at Augusta National with former world No.1 Tiger Woods returning to the scene of four previous triumphs.

At 42, 14-time Major champion Woods still manages to capture the hearts and minds of golf fans across the world with even his rivals on the course content with willing the American icon to what would be a monumental victory.

Woods, a year after back surgery saved his carer, has not played in a Major since 2015 - and has not won one since 2008.

But can he conjure the magic that elevated him to superstardom and celebrate his fifth Green Jacket come Sunday evening?

That remains to be seen, of course. But Woods begins his quest playing alongside England's in-form Tommy Fleetwood and Australia's Marc Leishman.

The trio tee off at 3.42pm (UK time) and we will bring you the latest updates and reaction right here.
