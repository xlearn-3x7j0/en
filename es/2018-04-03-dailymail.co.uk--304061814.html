Manchester United's stars arrived for training as preparations begin for a crucial game against rivals Manchester City on Saturday. 

Jose Mourinho was chauffeur driven in and his players pulled up to their Carrington base for Tuesday's session.

Jesse Lingard was behind the wheel of his Bentley and Romelu Lukaku drove his black Rolls Royce with a red trim.

Jesse Lingard wore glasses and a hoodie as he pulled up to training in his Bentley

The England and Manchester United winger has been in sensational form recently 

Jose Mourinho was chauffeur driven and checked his phone in the back seat 

Nemanja Matic pulled up in a Mini, a more understated ride than his team-mates, and seemed to be whistling a tune to himself, in good spirits after the 2-0 victory over Swansea.  

Mourinho will be drilling his side with the gameplan to thwart City and prevent the embarrassment of allowing United's fierce rivals from clinching the Premier League title.

The Old Trafford club trail Pep Guardiola's brilliant side by a massive 16 points in the table and haven't been able to keep up with the noisy neighbours. 

But an upset would still be hugely satisfying for Mourinho's men who have come in for criticism this season, having been dumped out of the Champions League by Sevilla and produced some underwhelming displays.

United will also be boosted by the fact that City have to play Liverpool at Anfield on Wednesday. 

The first leg of their Champions League quarter-final will potentially leave Guardiola's key players fatigued for the visit of their old foes.

With the season entering it's final chapter, victory at the Etihad and an FA Cup triumph would add much-needed gloss to United's campaign.
