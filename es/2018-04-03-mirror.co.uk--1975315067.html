Manny Pacquiao confirms opponent and date for next fight

Manny Pacquiao has confirmed he will return to the ring against Lucas Matthysse on July 15 in Malaysia.

The ring legend has not fought since he was surprisingly outpointed by Jeff Horn in Brisbane last summer.

He will attempt to win back a version of the world welterweight title when he challenges for Matthysee's WBA belt.

But Pacquiao is set to do so without long-time trainer Freddie Roach who has been in his corner since 2001.

Instead, the Filipino will be trained by friend and assistant trainer Buboy Fernandez.

Pacquiao's adviser Michael Koncz said: "That's Manny's decision. Manny has told me Buboy will be handling the training on this fight.

"What's important to Manny is that he has one voice to listen to in the corner that he trusts, and I guess that's Buboy for this fight."

Pacquiao had beaten Timothy Bradley and Jessie Vargas following his 2015 defeat by Floyd Mayweather.

But he was upset by Horn who won a unanimous decision at the Suncorp Stadium to rip Pacquiao's WBO belt from him.
