Shocking video shows robbers shoot at Pizza Hut delivery driver

  • This is the moment two suspects are seen shooting at a Pizza Hut delivery driver
  • The horrific incident happened in Magnolia on Tuesday just before 2pm
  • The 22-year-old delivery driver had received a prank call for pizza beforehand 
  • The suspects fled the scene. Police are asking the public for help in finding them 

Published: 22:45 EDT, 4 April 2018 | Updated: 06:41 EDT, 5 April 2018

A 22-year-old Pizza Hut driver received a prank order before she was shot at by two suspects near the home she attempted to deliver to on Tuesday.

The horrific incident happened in Magnolia, Texas, just before 2pm, police say. Authorities released dash cam footage from the victim's vehicle.

She had been driving from a home where she had attempted to make a delivery but was told by the resident they hadn't placed an order. 

The woman got back in the vehicle and drove away, and while on Highland Boulevard, she spotted a black SUV block the roadway in front of her.

The Montgomery County Sheriff's Office in Texas released dash cam footage showing the moment two suspects shot at a Pizza Hut delivery driver

Deputies were dispatched to the 29500 block of Tanglewood Road, Magnolia on Tuesday

That's when two suspects, who she said tried to rob her, got out of the SUV. She saw one carrying the gun in hand before the shot was fired.

The pizza delivery driver put her car in reverse and backed up as quickly as possible to safety.

She then hid at a stranger's house where she phoned 911 for help.

The Montgomery County Sheriff's Office revealed they found a shell casing from the scene where the shooting happened.

Police said in a statement: 'The Tahoe contained a least three occupants, two of the occupants exited from the passenger side wearing masks.'

'From the video, the Tahoe’s license plate appeared to be covered with a piece of clothing,' police said.

The suspects then fled. Police are asking anyone with further information to contact their department or send tips to Crime Stoppers.
