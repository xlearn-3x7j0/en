Juventus vs Real Madrid, LIVE: Follow all the Champions League action

The Champions League returns with a bang for the quarter-finals as Juventus take on Real Madrid in a repeat of last season's final at the Principality Stadium.

For Juventus, who saw off Tottenham Hotspur in the previous round, the tie provides an opportunity for revenge following their 4-1 defeat in the Welsh capital 10 months ago.

Despite a poor season domestically, Madrid continue to shine in Europe and Zinedine Zidane's will be confident of getting a result at the Allianz Stadium in their pursuit of a third straight Champions League title. Follow all the action as it happens.
