Liverpool arrive at Melwood as City train at Everton

Liverpool stars arrived at training ahead of Wednesday night’s Champions League quarter-final clash with Manchester City - who took a morning session at Everton’s Goodison Park.

The Reds welcome the Premier League champions-elect to Anfield hoping to record a positive result before next week’s Etihad tie.

Forward stars Mohamed Salah and Roberto Firmino were pictured en route to the light session at the club’s Melwood training ground.

Nathaniel Clyne was also pictured, and he’ll have to be on top form should Liverpool keep out Pep Guardiola’s side, with the Reds amidst a defensive crisis.

Joel Matip , Ragnar Klavan, Joe Gomez and potential centre-back Emre Can are all out of the game, leaving Virgil van Dijk and Dejan Lovren as Jurgen Klopp’s only fit senior centre-backs.

And the German knows it’ll be tough to keep free-scoring City at bay, but plans to attack from the off to achieve the right result.

“Yet we are difficult to defend against, too,” he said ahead of the game. “You give us the chance to play one pass and it is very difficult to stop the next one. We either miss the chance or we score.

“You have no chance. But we can. You need to have a plan for your players and then it is a mix of quality and ­character. We all have to use the skills of the players.”

Manchester City stars also touched down on Merseyside and they’ll train at Reds’ rivals Everton’s Goodison Park ahead of the showdown.
