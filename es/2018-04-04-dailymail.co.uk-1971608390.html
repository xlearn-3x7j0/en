Entire contents of Heathrow's old Terminal 1 goes up for auction

Those in the market for security scanners, baggage carousels and check-in desks may have hit the jackpot. 

The entire contents of Heathrow's old Terminal 1 are set to go up for auction later this month on Saturday, April 21. 

Prospective buyers will be able to bid on a piece of 'iconic aviation history' when they peruse the lot filled with 110 check-in desks, security cameras and relics of the terminal. 

For bidders looking for something more suited to the home, there are 4,000 rows of airport seating up for grabs and 10 pieces of art by the late Polish-born painter Stefan Knapp.
