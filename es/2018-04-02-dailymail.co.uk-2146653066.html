Arsenal's Bellerin responds to Ozil with hilarious tweet

  • Mesut Ozil was angry with Hector Bellerin during Arsenal's win over Stoke
  • Ozil was frustrated after Bellerin passed to him in an offside position 
  • Bellerin responded to the incident after the game with a hilarious tweet  

Published: 06:46 EDT, 2 April 2018 | Updated: 11:09 EDT, 2 April 2018

Arsenal team-mates Hector Bellerin and Mesut Ozil exchanged words during the 3-0 victory over Stoke on Sunday but it was all smiles after the game with the Spaniard making light of the situation with a hilarious tweet.

During the game Ozil was seen berating Bellerin after he received the ball from the defender in an offside position.

Video footage suggested that Ozil told Bellerin that he speaks too much and the German indicated that he would deal with the matter in the dressing room. 

Hector Bellerin reacted with a hilarious tweet following his exchange with Mesut Ozil

Ozil wasn't happy with Bellerin but the situation between the Arsenal stars was soon sorted out

Ozil was livid after Bellerin passed the ball to him when he was in an offside position

Despite Ozil's frustration, the disagreement between the pair was quickly resolved

However, any fears of a serious issue between the pair were quickly dispelled.

Bellerin posted a tweet with a picture of himself in the dressing room accompanied by the caption 'When @MesutOzil1088 walks into the dressing room... love you bro'

Arsenal weren't at their best against Stoke, especially in the first half, but they still managed to prevail by a comfortable margin in the end, with January signing Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang hitting a brace, while Alexandre Lacazette marked his return from injury by scoring a penalty.

With a top four finish in the Premier League looking increasingly unlikely, Arsenal's main focus for the remainder of the season is on the Europa League, with Bellerin and Ozil both in line to start in Thursday night's quarter-final, first leg tie at home to CSKA Moscow.
