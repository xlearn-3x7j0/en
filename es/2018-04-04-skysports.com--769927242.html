LISTEN: The Offload Podcast - Champions Cup recap featuring Munster and Leinster's wins

By Sky Sports Rugby

Last Updated: 04/04/18 10:00pm

In the latest episode, James Gemmell and Will Greenwood discuss the quarter-finals of the Champions Cup including the European success of Irish duo Munster and Leinster.

Munster produced a magical finish to defeat Toulon while favourites Leinster's strength in depth is assessed after the semi-final lineup was finalised.

Scarlets coach Wayne Pivac has held informal talks about succeeding Wales boss Warren Gatland. The Offload looks at his credentials for the international role after guiding Scarlets to the PRO12 title last season.

Saracens owner Nigel Wray has confirmed his intention to buy the club outright from South African investors Remgro. What impact will this have on the Premiership club's future? Ex-Saracens director of rugby Mark Evans joins on the phone to talk about the state of English club rugby.

Dan Carter came off the bench in Racing 92's Champions Cup win at Clermont Auvergne. The Offload looks at his potential influence as Racing chase European glory - the only trophy Carter has yet to win in his career.
