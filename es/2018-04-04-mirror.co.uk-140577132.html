Arsenal fans turn on Wenger after Oxlade-Chamberlain scores stunner

Alex Oxlade-Chamberlain scored a stunner against Manchester City in Liverpool's Champions League quarter-final first leg at Anfield - and Arsenal fans used it as an excuse to turn on Arsene Wenger.

The England international traded the Emirates for Anfield last summer as part of a £35million transfer.

After a slow start, the 24-year-old is now strutting his stuff for Liverpool and is showing he can do it on Europe's biggest stage.

The Ox scored a long-range beauty against Manchester City to put the hosts 2-0 up on Wednesday.

After seeing their former player score a pivotal Champions League knockout goal, Arsenal fans began blaming Wenger for Oxlade-Chamberlain never producing the goods for them.

One Gunners supporter tweeted: "Oxlade-Chamberlain is the greatest proof that Wenger is a s*** coach today."

Another fan added: "This is the same Oxlade Chamberlain ? F*** Arsene Wenger."

Sadio Mane added to Liverpool's tally 10 minutes later.
