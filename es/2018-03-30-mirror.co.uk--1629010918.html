John Barnes reveals who thinks will win the Champions League

John Barnes has revealed who he thinks will win the Champions League this season, and given his opinion on Liverpool's tantalising clash with Manchester City.

The Reds thumped Porto 5-0 in the last-16 of this year's competition to set up a quarter-final clash with the Citizens, who beat FC Basel 5-2 on aggregate.

Elsewhere Barcelona are in action against Roma in their quarter-final match, while Sevilla face Bayern Munich and Real Madrid take on Juventus.

Speaking to Mirror Football, on behalf of Bonus Code Bets, about the teams still left in Europe's elite competition, Barnes revealed who his money is on to return from Kiev with the title this season.

"There's Barcelona, Real Madrid, Liverpool, Manchester City, Bayern Munich; they're all good teams," said the former Liverpool winger and England international.

"I think after this round, once we get to the semi-finals - of course Liverpool can beat Man City and also Man City can beat Liverpool, but I always make Barcelona favourites to win the Champions League.

"The quality that they have. The experience that they have also, makes them favourites for the Champions League."

The Champions League resumes on April 3, and the quarter-final ties will conclude eight days later.
