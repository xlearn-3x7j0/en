Angler finds bizarre fish with human teeth

  • A fish with teeth that look just like a human's has been found in Indonesia
  • The creature was given to Ahmad Seseray who intended to cook it for dinner 
  • When the pupil got it home he realised that the creature had pearly whites

Published: 06:00 EDT, 5 April 2018 | Updated: 12:46 EDT, 5 April 2018

An unusual sea creature, with teeth strikingly similar to that of a human, has been found in a small fishing town. 

It was caught in Kimindores, in the West Papua region of Indonesia, Yahoo reported.

It's thought the unusual sea creature is a Emperor fish a popular seafood in Indonesia and Australia 

A student, Ahmad Seseray, was given the fish by his school teacher who reportedly found it while fishing down by the town's docks.

He had intended to cook the fish with his family but plans changed after he opened up its mouth.  

Ahmad found the sea creature had square molar-type gnashers similar to those found in humans.

A video taken of the fish shows residents examining the bizarre creature while holding it's mouth agape to show off it's pearly whites.

The creature is believed to be an Emperor Fish.

Ahmad's mother, Hadijah, said the fish caused a stir in the remote village.

Footage shows the baffled locals in the town examining the fish while holding it mouth agape to show off it's human like teeth 

A pupil, Ahmad Seseray, was given the fish by his school teacher who reportedly found it while fishing

'I opened the mouth of the fish and looked carefully and my son was right, the fish had large teeth that were similar to the structure of human teeth,' she said. 

'Many people who came to hear the information of the discovery of a fish that has teeth similar to human teeth.'

Instead of cooking the strange animal, Hadijah decided to put it in her freezer.
