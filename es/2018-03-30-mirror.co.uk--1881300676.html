Jose Mourinho will be speaking ahead of his side's clash against Swansea on Friday.

Manchester United host the Welsh side at Old Trafford on Saturday for a rare 3pm kick off.

With no major injuries from the international break, the Red Devils will be looking to further cement their place in the Premier League top four against the Swans.

However, winning will be no formality with Swansea showing good form since Carlos Carvalhal took charge.

The Portuguese, who once studied under Mourinho at Chelsea, has guided Swansea out of the relegation zone up to 14th.

But Swansea are by no means safe just yet and will put up a fight in Manchester.

Welcome, who wants to talk Luke Shaw? Probably not Jose Mourinho

Luke Shaw confronted Jose Mourinho in the Manchester United changing room when he told him he was coming off against Brighton, according to reports.

The 22-year-old left-back has endured a start-stop career under the Portuguese at Old Trafford, but was selected ahead of makeshift wing-back Ashley Young for the FA Cup clash.

The Red Devils were 1-0 up going into the break against Brighton, thanks to an effort from Romelu Lukaku, but Mourinho was unhappy with what he had seen from Shaw.

He told him he would be replacing him with his regular left-back option, prompting Shaw to allegedly retaliate in frustration, and raise his voice.
