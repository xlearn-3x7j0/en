Boy, 11, filmed yodelling at Walmart becomes an internet sensation

An 11-year-old boy has gone viral after a video showing him yodelling at Walmart was shared online. 

Mason Ramsey, from Golconda, Illinois, is seen belting out his rendition of Hank Williams' Lovesick Blues in the clip, while wearing a white shirt and bow tie.

The sweet video has already been watched millions of times, capturing the hearts of fans all over the world - with some declaring it the 'best thing they've seen'. 

Mason now has his very own Twitter account, where he's thanked fans for their support, as well as sharing other videos of himself yodelling. 

He is also set to perform his first concert at his local Walmart store, after becoming an internet sensation.
