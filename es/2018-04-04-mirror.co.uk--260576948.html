Manchester United 'eye £30m deal for Juan Mata replacement'

Chelsea winger Willian is reportedly set to replace Juan Mata at Manchester United.

Jose Mourinho wanted a reunion with Willian last summer and held talks but the Brazilian opted to remain at Stamford Bridge.

But with Chelsea set to miss out on next season's Champions League, United are confident of getting the deal done, reports the Sun.

Mata could be the man to make way at Old Trafford this summer, with his contract expiring next summer.

Mata remains hopeful of signing a new deal but he could well make way.
