FOX Sports Southwest will televise 18 Dallas Wings games in 2018

ARLINGTON, TX – The Dallas Wings have announced a new agreement with FOX Sports Southwest for the 2018 season. All 2018 Dallas Wings home games will be broadcast live on FOX Sports Southwest throughout the majority of Texas and Oklahoma, as well as in portions of Arkansas and Louisiana. Games will be televised on either FOX Sports Southwest or FOX Sports Southwest Plus.

“We want to thank FOX Sports Southwest for their continued partnership and support,” said President and CEO Greg Bibb. “We are thrilled Wings broadcast will reach parts of four states. We are excited that fans throughout the region will get to see the best professional women’s basketball players in the world throughout the summer.”

FOX Sports will televise 18 games, the most in Wings history. Sixteen of the 18 games slated will be broadcast on Fox Sports Southwest Plus, with the other two on Fox Sports Southwest.

Every Wings telecast on FOX Sports Southwest and FOX Sports Southwest Plus will be available on FOX Sports GO, the live streaming platform that showcases FOX Sports’ events and studio programming through pay-TV providers.

FOX Sports GO is available on FOXSportsGO.com or on the free FOX Sports GO app, which can be downloaded from iTunes, Google Play, Amazon App Store and Windows Store. Streaming is also now available on several connected devices, including Apple TV, Android TV, FireTV and stick, Roku, Chromecast, Xbox One and YouTube TV.

Regular-season coverage on FOX Sports Southwest begins on Sunday, May 20 at 2 p.m. CT with the home opener against the Atlanta Dream. Coverage will include pre-game interviews and an in-depth look at the 2018 Wings roster.

Ron Thulin will return for his third season as play-by-play announcer and will be joined by TCU head coach Raegan Pebley, who is also in her third season as the primary analyst. Bob Steinfeld will serve as producer. This season marks his second season leading the broadcast. For complete regional sports coverage, telecast schedules and channel listings, visit FOXSportsSouthwest.com.

About the Dallas Wings The Dallas Wings are one of twelve professional teams in the Women’s National Basketball Association (WNBA). The team, now in season three, was introduced to the Dallas-Fort Worth area on November 2, 2015, becoming the first WNBA organization to call the Dallas Metroplex home. On May 19, 2016, the team tipped off their inaugural season inside College Park Center, a state-of-the-art facility located on the campus of the University of Texas at Arlington. The Wings compete against the best women’s basketball teams in the world each summer, playing a 34-game WNBA slate beginning in May and ending in August.

All games televised on FOX Sports Southwest and FOX Sports Southwest PLUS also will be streamed live on FOX Sports GO. FOX Sports GO is available to customers of participating pay-TV providers on iOS, Android, Fire tablets and Fire phones, Amazon Fire TV and Fire TV Stick, select Windows devices and online at FOXSportsGO.com. Fans can download the mobile app for free from the iTunes App Store, Google Play, Amazon App Store and Windows Store.
