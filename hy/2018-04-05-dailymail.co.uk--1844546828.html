NBA 2K League Draft: All of the picks by professional NBA teams

  • From 70,000 applicants, 102 people are now professional NBA 2K18 players
  • They were selected by esports teams representing actual NBA organisations
  • Most will be on a salary of at least $35,000, with $1 million prize money on offer 

Published: 11:42 EDT, 5 April 2018 | Updated: 11:43 EDT, 5 April 2018

70,000 people tried to get into the NBA 2K League, but only 102 were good enough to make it.  

These 102 are the best in the world at the NBA 2K18 video game, and last night in Madison Square Garden, they were drafted by 17 real life NBA teams to represent them in a brand new esports league.

Mavs Gaming, the esports team of the Dallas Mavericks, had the number one overall pick, and selected Point Guard Artreyo 'Dimez' Boyd from Ohio. The pick was announced by NBA commissioner Adam Silver.

The first pick in the NBA 2K League Draft went to the Mavs who chose Artreyo 'Dimez' Boyd

The players picked in the first five rounds will all receive salaries of at least $35,000, while the rest will earn $32,000. There's also a prize pool of $1 million for the entire league. 

Now that the teams are all in place, they will have a few weeks to practice before the NBA 2K League starts in May.
