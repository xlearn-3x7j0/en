Are these the worst life hacks ever?

  • People from around the world share their life hacks to a thread on Diply
  • Some showed off dangerous methods of giving everyday items new uses
  • Others revealed they had chosen to be creative in order to save money 

Published: 06:03 EDT, 4 April 2018 | Updated: 16:29 EDT, 4 April 2018

Trying to cut time and save money is a priority to most people, but at what cost?

A thread of life hacks collated on social site Diply reveal how some solutions could prove more harmful than clever - and people should probably steer clear of trying to recreate them.

The photos show off dangerous methods of cooking, travelling and upgrading household items on a budget.

One person shared how they attempted to achieve the illusion of a trendy fireplace by placing a tank of gas underneath wooden furniture, complete with logs despite the impending danger. 

Others revealed hazardous money saving hacks for giving gadgets a new use.

Here, FEMAIL rounds up the most shocking examples.  

People from across their globe contributed their questionable life hacks to a thread on social site Diply. One person revealed how they attempted to create their own indoor fireplace

Another person revealed they had stolen a supermarket trolley to complete their makeshift barbecue 

A man shared how he had found another use for a car logo as a plate for perfect portions

Aspiring to join a motorbike gang, one person replaced the front wheels of their lawnmower with a spare part 

Without fear of danger, another person tried to create a water slide using a childhood toy

Cutting cost on a humidifier, one person carefully placed a fan on their toilet seat

Another person attempted to keep their plant exactly as they had placed it by attaching a rock

One person revealed their frustration with flies by demonstrating how they had inserted spikes into their swatter

A lazy individual shared how they had incorporated hand washing while using the urinal to reduce time in the bathroom

Fed up of buying microphones, a gamer shared how they had plugged a house phone into their remote control 

Another person revealed they decided to make life difficult for whoever kept using their ladder by placing it within a fence

One person decided to risk harming themselves rather than struggle to slice their bagel 

Opting not to replace their electric whisk, another person risked hurting themselves by blending ingredients with a drill
