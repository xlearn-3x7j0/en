Bayern Munich will be hoping to keep up their stunning form when they travel to Sevilla for the first leg of their UEFA Champions League quarter-final at the Ramon Sanchez Pizjuan Stadium.

The German side have been in stunning form having beaten Borussia Dortmund 6-0 at the weekend to move on the cusp of retaining the Bundesliga, and they travel to Spain having hammered Besiktas 8-1 on aggregate in the last-16.

Sevilla however will show little fear of their opponents having caused the biggest shock of the last-16 by knocking out Manchester United. Follow all the action as it happens.
