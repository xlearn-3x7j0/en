Man empties out a bottle of Coca-Cola and finds a decaying DEAD MOUSE

  • Diego Pereya, from Freyre in Argentina, holds the apparently sealed bottle
  • He says he has noticed something inside and begins to pour out the fizzy drink
  • When the contents of the bottle has been emptied what appears to be a dead mouse is left 

Published: 17:04 EDT, 2 April 2018 | Updated: 17:09 EDT, 2 April 2018

This is the revolting moment a man claims he found a dead mouse floating around in his bottle of Coca-Cola.

Diego Pereya, from Freyre in Argentina, holds the apparently sealed bottle of the fizzy drink up to the camera.

He then says that he spotted something unusual in the bottle and decided to film whatever he discovered.

Mr Pereya opens the bottle and pours the Coca-Cola into a yellow bucket and once it is empty he makes a shocking discovery.

Diego Pereya points to the unusual object he has noticed in his bottle of Coca-Cola and then begins pouring the fizzy drink out of the bottle

At the bottom of the bottle is a decaying mouse.   

Pereyra says: 'There it is. All those hairs (that are stuck to the bottle) are from the little mouse.

'There is the mouse, people. Stop drinking Coca-Cola.'

As the Coca-Cola gushes out of the bottle the mouse begins to appear and evidence of its fur can be seen on the side of the plastic bottle

Some netizens were disgusted at the startling video, with user 'Pablo Aquino' commenting: 'It is the second video I have seen where a mouse comes out of a Coca-Cola bottle, what is going on?

'Is the mouse part of the secret recipe?'

Coca-Cola have not commented on the video which has been shared over 900 times on social media.     

The video was posted the online where it has received over 20,000 views in just two days.

The cameraman then shows the dead mouse as it is caught in the top of the bottle
