Zidane's priceless reaction to Ronaldo's incredible goal against Juventus

Zinedine Zidane simply could not believe what Cristiano Ronaldo produced in Real Madrid's Champions League victory at Juventus on Tuesday night.

Ronaldo - who had already found the net in just the third minute of the game - launched into an incredible 64th minute overhead kick from a Dani Carvajal cross to double Real's lead and even lead the Juve fans to applaud the goal.

Left-back Marcelo later made it 3-0 to almost completely kill off the quarter-final tie, leaving boss Zinedine Zidane delighted, but eyes were drawn to how the Frenchman had reacted Ronaldo's earlier piece of genius.

Because it was priceless.

Zidane - who was no stranger to a brilliant Champions League goal or two in his time - simply couldn't believe what his main man had done.

The Real boss's reaction was a big hit on social media, where fans enjoyed how he'd seemingly been left baffled.
