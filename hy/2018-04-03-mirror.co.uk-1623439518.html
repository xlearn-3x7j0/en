Lewandowski's WAG reveals how he celebrated record 5-goal scoring spree

The wife of Bayern Munich hitman Robert Lewandowski has revealed how he celebrated scoring a record five goals in nine minutes back in 2015.

The Pole received four Guinness world record certificates, including the most goals scored by a substitute in the Bundesliga and the fastest Bundesliga hat-trick.

At the time the 29-year-old said: “It is a great honour to have my achievements on the pitch hit the Guinness Book of Records.”

But did that get to his head? Not quite and his wife, Anna, revealed just what he did to celebrate the famous feat.

She said: "When Robert scored five goals in nine minutes he was not really satisfied and he was complaining that it was possible for him to score a sixth. After that he started cleaning the floors at home."

The Karate expert and her friend Marek were talking about how the Bayern forward acts after goal feats, and it turns out he's pretty normal about it all.

"After the famous match with Real Madrid when he scored four goals, everybody was extremely excited," family friend Marek said.

"But he just came back home and simply asked for some pancakes. He was asking me why I did not turn on the dishwasher."

The striker has been in superb form once notching 35 goals in 38 matches for the Bundesliga champions this season.

He's yet to commit to the Bundesliga champions with reports rife he will join La Liga giants Real Madrid for a cut-price fee this summer.
